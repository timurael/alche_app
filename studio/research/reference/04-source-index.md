## Source Reliability Notes

- GWS, GWI, McKinsey, BCG, WEF — Institutional research, high reliability
- NielsenIQ — Consumer survey data, high reliability
- Sacra, ecdb, Owler — Revenue estimates, moderate reliability (private company data)
- Fueler, Entrepreneur — Business journalism, moderate reliability
- IGNANT — Design/culture editorial, reliable for brand positioning context
- Meetup/Reddit — Community size data, snapshot-in-time (may change)
- Wearable abandonment / app retention — Widely cited but exact methodology varies by source; treat as directional
