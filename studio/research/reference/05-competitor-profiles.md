# Competitor Profiles — Key Brands

Brief profiles of the most relevant competitors from the positioning analysis.

---

## Bottom-Right Quadrant: Optimization-First

### Blueprint (Bryan Johnson)
- **Position:** Science 9.5, Humanity 1.0, Inclusion 1.5, Integration 2/6
- **What they do:** Extreme longevity protocol ("Don't Die"), biomarker-obsessed
- **Revenue/Funding:** $60M raise (Source: Athletech News, Longevity-Germany)
- **Why they're not Alche:** Highest science score but lowest humanity. Hyper-masculine, protocol-rigid, inaccessible. The anti-thesis of "imperfect longevity."

### Function Health
- **Position:** Science 8.5, Humanity 3.0, Inclusion 3.0, Integration 3/6
- **What they do:** 100+ biomarker testing platform
- **Why they're not Alche:** Strong clinical depth but clinical positioning only. No lifestyle integration, no community, no physical space.

### WHOOP
- **Position:** Science 7.5, Humanity 2.5, Inclusion 2.0, Integration 3/6
- **What they do:** Performance wearable (HRV, recovery, strain)
- **Why they're not Alche:** Male athlete core audience. Data-heavy, no warmth. Part of the retention crisis (90% abandonment).

### Ultrahuman
- **Position:** Science 8.0, Humanity 2.5, Inclusion 2.0, Integration 3/6
- **What they do:** CGM-based metabolic tracking, smart ring
- **Why they're not Alche:** Tech-forward language, narrow demographic. Same "optimization-first" positioning trap.

## Top-Left Quadrant: Lifestyle Wellness

### Goop
- **Position:** Science 2.5, Humanity 7.0, Inclusion 7.0, Integration 3/6
- **What they do:** Gwyneth Paltrow's lifestyle/wellness brand
- **Why they're not Alche:** Strong brand humanity and inclusivity but scientifically unserious. Known for pseudoscience controversies.

### Erewhon
- **Position:** Science 1.5, Humanity 7.5, Inclusion 5.0, Integration 3/6
- **What they do:** Premium grocery as wellness lifestyle brand (LA)
- **Why they're not Alche:** Pure aspiration play. No health outcomes, no data, no personalization.

### Moon Juice
- **Position:** Science 2.0, Humanity 6.5, Inclusion 6.0, Integration 2/6
- **What they do:** Adaptogen supplements and skincare
- **Why they're not Alche:** Aesthetic strength without clinical grounding. Products without platform.

## Category Inversion Precedents

### Liquid Death
- **Position:** Science 0.5, Humanity 9.0, Inclusion 7.5, Integration 1/6
- **What they do:** Water in tallboy cans with punk/death metal branding
- **Revenue:** $333M (2024), $1.4B valuation (Sources: Fueler, Sacra, Entrepreneur)
- **Relevance to Alche:** Proof that inverting a serious category with rebellious positioning creates outsized value. They did it to water. Alche does it to longevity.

### MUD\WTR
- **Position:** Science 2.0, Humanity 8.5, Inclusion 6.5, Integration 1/6
- **What they do:** Mushroom coffee alternative, anti-hustle-culture positioning
- **Revenue:** ~$47M (2024 est.) (Sources: ecdb, Owler)
- **Relevance to Alche:** Anti-optimization positioning in a commoditized category. Similar ethos but no longevity depth.

## Berlin Competitors

### ANTI Berlin
- **Position:** Science 3.0, Humanity 8.0, Inclusion 5.5, Integration 2/6
- **What they do:** Longevity + culture hybrid space on Brunnenstraße
- **Size:** 20K Instagram followers, single location (Sources: IGNANT, antispaces.com)
- **Relevance:** Validates Berlin demand for science-meets-lifestyle. But limited scope (no app, no products, no membership model).

### Daluma Berlin
- **Position:** Science 2.5, Humanity 7.0, Inclusion 5.0, Integration 2/6
- **What they do:** Health food cafe/restaurant
- **Relevance:** General wellness, no longevity specificity. More lifestyle than science.

## Closest Competitors (Most Overlap with Alche)

### Oura
- **Position:** Science 7.0, Humanity 4.0, Inclusion 4.5, Integration 3/6
- **Why closest:** Best balance of science + some brand warmth in the wearable space. But still a device company, not a lifestyle platform.

### Parsley Health
- **Position:** Science 7.0, Humanity 5.0, Inclusion 6.0, Integration 1/6
- **Why closest:** Functional medicine + women-friendly positioning. But clinical-only (no physical space, no community, no products).

### Life Time
- **Position:** Science 4.0, Humanity 5.5, Inclusion 5.5, Integration 4/6
- **Why closest:** Highest integration score among competitors (4/6). Physical space + app + membership + products. But not longevity-specific, not science-deep.
