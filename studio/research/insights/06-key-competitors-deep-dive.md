## InsideTracker

| Metric | Value | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Founded | 2009, Cambridge, MA | [Crunchbase](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/segterra-insidetracker) |
| Founder | Dr. Gil Blander (MIT postdoc, Guarente lab) | [InsideTracker Team](https://www.insidetracker.com/team) |
| Advisory Board | David Sinclair (Harvard, chairman), Leonard Guarente (MIT) | [The Org](https://theorg.com/org/insidetracker/org-chart/david-sinclair) |
| Total raised | $17.5-19M (Series B Sept 2022, no new rounds since) | [Crunchbase](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/segterra-insidetracker/company_financials) |
| Est. annual revenue | ~$3.8M (Owler estimate, unconfirmed) | [Owler](https://www.owler.com/company/insidetracker) |
| Cumulative customers | 100,000+ | Company marketing |
| Trustpilot | 4.1/5 from 617 reviews. Bimodal: 70% 5-star, 13% 1-star | [Trustpilot](https://www.trustpilot.com/review/insidetracker.com) |
| Pricing | $340-589/test + $149/yr membership | [InsideTracker Store](https://store.insidetracker.com/) |
| Ultrahuman partnership | Jan 2025, blood data integration | [GlobeNewsWire](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2025/01/15/3010193/0/en/Ultrahuman-and-InsideTracker-Partner-to-Improve-Cardiovascular-Health-of-U-S-Consumers.html) |

**Key user complaints:** "Data without implementation" -- identifies problems but sends users away without a plan. Recommendations are "generic lists." App is buggy. DNA analysis is a black box. Expensive for episodic use.

**MIT connection is real but nuanced:** Blander did his postdoc at MIT under Guarente. Guarente sits on advisory board. MIT as an institution did not fund or back the company. It's "founded by MIT-trained scientists," not "MIT-backed."

| Raised | $14M total ($4M seed + $10M Series A from General Catalyst) | [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2025/10/30/bevel-raises-10m-series-a-from-general-catalyst-for-its-ai-health-companion/) |

**Key insight:** Bevel proves the integration thesis works commercially in 2025-2026. AI-powered health companion unifying wearable data with persistent memory. But purely digital -- no physical space, no community beyond the app, no biomarker testing.

## Humanity Inc

| Metric | Value | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Founded | 2019 | [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2020/08/20/humanity-inc-raises-funding-to-allow-us-to-monitor-and-affect-our-rate-of-aging/) |
| Raised | ~$5M+ | [Wefunder](https://wefunder.com/humanity) |
| Total users | 170,000+ | Wefunder, marketing materials |
| MAU | 20,000 | Wefunder disclosures |
| Premium conversion | 10% | Wefunder disclosures |
| Pricing | $180/yr | [Humanity Health](https://www.humanity.health/) |
| Advisory board | George Church (Harvard), Aubrey de Grey | Company site |

**Key accuracy problems:** Users report biological age calculations are "totally off the mark" based on limited inputs (steps + HRV). Implausible results that contradict clinical assessments. Limited data ingestion from wearables. Fundamental validation challenge: no established gold standard for real-time biological age from wearable data alone.

Sources: [Decrypt](https://decrypt.co/316462/turn-back-time-apps-reverse-biological-age); [JustUseApp Reviews](https://justuseapp.com/en/app/1519091344/humanity-stay-healthy-longer/reviews)
