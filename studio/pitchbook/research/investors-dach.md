## Top 5 for Alche

1. **Vorwerk Ventures** — Their public thesis reads like Alche was written into it: "Consumer, Food, Health, Climate." Berlin-based, consumer DNA (HelloFresh, Everdrop, Flaschenpost), and their parent Vorwerk Group is a direct-selling powerhouse that gets content-to-commerce intuitively.
2. **Heartcore Capital** — Explicit "health & wellness" sector focus, portfolio proof in Kaia Health, and 60 of 104 companies are B2C. Berlin office, pre-seed willing at €1.5M+, and they already bet on consumer brand stories.
3. **Cherry Ventures** — Berlin HQ, Zalando/Spotify operator DNA (founder-first retail/content instincts), lists consumer + health tech explicitly, and Sophia Bendz is a perfect partner match for content-to-commerce thesis.
4. **Calm/Storm Ventures (Vienna)** — Tiny checks but they are the European pre-seed healthtech/tabootech leader. Queer founder + longevity + taboo-breaking wellness is their exact sweet spot; top-10 FemTech investor globally.
5. **Atlantic Labs** — First-cheque speed (€25K–€1M), explicit "Health & Wellbeing" focus, Berlin-based, and Christophe Maire is famously founder-first with fast decisions. Great for the first €200–300K slice.

## Notes on Fund Landscape for Alche

- **The Berlin/DACH pre-seed consumer fund pool is genuinely narrow.** Only 4-5 funds (Vorwerk, Heartcore, Shio, Cherry, Calm/Storm) have consumer wellness as an actual thesis. The rest are "we sometimes do consumer" — meaning you'll be an atypical deal and need strong comps.
- **Xiaohongshu as a comp is likely unfamiliar in DACH.** Always pair it with AG1 ($600M), Ritual, Thrive Market, and Kaia Health (Berlin exit) for local resonance.
- **Germany's €7.2B supplements market is a non-obvious edge.** Most DACH VCs underestimate the category size; lead your pitch with the Sunday Natural / BRAINEFFECT comps which are known to German investors.
- **BRAINEFFECT (acquired by Schwabe Pharma in 2025) deliberately avoided VC.** This will come up — be ready with why Alche's AI-first approach changes the math vs. pure-CPG.
- **Her.one (her1) is the cleanest Berlin comp.** €5.5M Series A led by Five Seasons Ventures with Rheingau Founders + IBB Ventures. Those three names deserve direct outreach as they've already bet on this exact thesis.
- **Female/queer founder angle is a real advantage** through Auxxo, Calm/Storm, Female Founders Fund F, and IBB Ventures (which backed her1).
- **Language:** English is broadly accepted by all 20 funds. IBB Ventures + Freigeist skew most German-first on paperwork. Founder is Berlin-based which resolves any "why DE?" question immediately.
