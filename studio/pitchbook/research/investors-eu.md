# Alche — EU Pre-Seed VC Research (Nordics, France, Iberia, Italy, Netherlands, BeNeLux, DACH-adjacent)

**Purpose.** Identify pan-EU or sub-region-specialist pre-seed VCs with explicit wellness / longevity / femtech / consumer-health theses who can write €150K–€500K into Alche's €500K pre-seed round.

**Alche, in one line.** Berlin-based "Xiaohongshu for longevity in Europe" — free AI longevity app that drives curated supplement commerce (3P today, own-brand later) with a thin €5–9/mo membership tier.

**Comps investors will recognize.** Xiaohongshu (content-to-commerce), AG1 (single-hero supplement), The Ordinary / DECIEM (ingredient-transparent retail), Thrive Market (curated wellness marketplace), Ritual (clinical consumer supplement), Costco (proof point of curation-with-loyalty).

**Research date.** April 2026. All funds verified active in 2025 or 2026 via Tracxn / CBInsights / Crunchbase / own-site signals.

---

## Method & scoring rubric

**Fit score** (1–10) weighs: (a) explicit wellness / consumer-health thesis, (b) true pre-seed check appetite at €150K–€500K, (c) Europe-native mandate that fits a Berlin / solo founder, (d) portfolio adjacency without direct conflict, (e) retail / DTC / supplement literacy.

**Red-flag conventions.** Conflict = fund already backs a direct competitor (AG1, Ritual, Heights, Form, Exhale, Feel, Vitl, Equi, Oura, Whoop, Hims, Biocol). Stage risk = fund's real sweet spot is seed / Series A, not €500K pre-seed. Geography risk = mandate excludes Berlin / Germany.
