## 1. Ada Ventures

- **Website:** https://www.adaventures.com (verified, active)
- **Check:** £250k–£1.5m at pre-seed, often leads 70%+ of rounds
- **Fund size:** £63m / $80m Fund II (final closed), total £100m+ AUM across two funds
- **Thesis:** "Investing in the best, not just the best-connected." Three themes: climate equity, economic empowerment, and **healthy ageing**. Their public-facing materials frame healthy ageing around cognitive preservation, preventative medicine, and the shift from reactive to predictive care — almost a word-for-word match for Alche's pitch.
- **Consumer/wellness fit:** **Yes.** They have explicitly hosted a "Health Equity dinner" around the healthy ageing thesis. Recent healthy aging check: **Aerska** (neurological disease prevention, Ada was first institutional check). Also Toothfairy (DTC dental), Medly AI, Interhuman AI, Juno Bio.
- **Key partners:**
  - **Check Warner (MBE)** — co-founding partner, diversity/inclusion focus, formerly Downing Ventures. Very public, active on LinkedIn.
  - **Matt Penneycard** — co-founder, 15+ years pre-seed, former head of Downing Ventures, focus on founder coaching.
- **Intro vector:** They publicly commit to reviewing cold inbound; strong intros via Atomico, Molten, BBB network. Also: Charles Hudson (Precursor) is on their steering committee and runs periodic London founder sessions.
- **Red flags for Alche:** None material. Ada actively seeks European founders (Berlin-based is fine). Only potential friction: they lean female-forward/underrepresented-founder; Alche is male solo founder but the healthy-aging thesis alignment likely outweighs.
- **Fit score: 10/10** — the closest single-thesis match in the UK ecosystem.
