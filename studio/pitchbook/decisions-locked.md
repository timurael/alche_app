## What the investor sees differently in v6

**Before (v5):** "We're building a longevity SaaS with 12-15x LTV:CAC and 85% gross margin."

**After (v6):** "We're building editorial commerce for longevity in Europe. Our content is free. Our retail is the business. The math works because membership funds operations (Costco) and content drives purchase (Xiaohongshu). Day-one revenue, not 23-month payback."

The story stops pretending to be SaaS. It starts being what it actually is — and the comps are bigger, the market is more European, the unit economics are honest.
