## KEY CONSIDERATIONS FOR ALCHE

### GmbH Structure
- YC, EF, and some US programs require corporate restructuring (Delaware flip)
- European programs (Techstars Berlin, Antler, SpinLab, Seedcamp) work with GmbH
- Factor legal costs of restructuring (~EUR 5-15K) into any US program decision

### Pre-Product Stage
- SpinLab, Antler, Reaktor.Berlin, Techstars all accept pre-product
- Seedcamp invests pre-product if team is exceptional
- YC explicitly welcomes pre-product teams
- Founders Factory and Plug & Play prefer more traction

### Consumer Wellness / Longevity Positioning
- Most Berlin accelerators lean Deep Tech / B2B
- Frame Alche as "health-tech" or "preventive health" rather than "wellness" for tech accelerators
- "Longevity science platform" sounds more investable than "lifestyle platform" in accelerator contexts
- Content-first GTM is unusual for accelerators — emphasize the platform/product vision

### Stacking Strategy
- Non-dilutive programs (SpinLab, Reaktor, Berlin Startup School) can be done alongside or before equity programs
- Apply to multiple non-dilutive programs simultaneously
- Use non-dilutive funding to build MVP, then apply to equity programs with more traction
