## Specialist Recommendation: Option B (Vertical Integration), supported by Option A (Curation Authority)

**Rationale:** Integration is the only moat framing that's both verifiable at pre-seed (the competitive map with 0 EU integrated players is a fact, not a claim) and structurally strengthened by the retail pivot (products fund the flywheel, every other layer compounds it). Curation authority is the strongest near-term proof point — within 6 months you can show research depth, product reviews, and repeat purchase data. Community identity should run as a through-line across the entire narrative rather than leading as the moat argument, because it's the hardest to prove before you have traction.
