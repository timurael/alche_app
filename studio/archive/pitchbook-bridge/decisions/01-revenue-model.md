# Revenue Model -- Decision Required

## Options

- [ ] **Option A: Pure Retail Commerce** -- No membership fee. 100% revenue from product sales (own-brand + curated). Simplest model, lowest friction, but weakest investor narrative and no behavioral lock-in.
- [ ] **Option B: Thin Membership + Retail (Costco Model)** -- EUR 7/mo membership unlocks 15% subscribe-and-save and community features. Membership is the profit center, products are the revenue engine. Proven by Costco (73% of gross profit from membership), Prime (2.3x spend uplift), and Thrive Market ($437/yr per member).
- [ ] **Option C: Freemium + Retail + Premium** -- Free app, product retail for everyone, EUR 29/mo premium tier for digital twin/CGM/doctor reviews. Three revenue streams but three things to optimize on a pre-seed team.

## Specialist Recommendation: Option B (Thin Membership + Retail)

**Rationale:** The Costco model is the highest-conviction choice because the behavioral economics are verified at scale (92.9% renewal, 2.3x spend uplift, 73% of gross profit from membership), the EUR 7/mo price point pays for itself on a single EUR 47 order at 15% discount, and the investor narrative shifts from 1-2x e-commerce multiples to 3-5x commerce + recurring revenue territory. Layer on the premium tier in Phase 2 once digital twin and CGM are shipping.
