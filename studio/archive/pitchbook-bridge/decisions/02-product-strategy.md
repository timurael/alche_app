# Product Strategy — Decision Required

## Options
- [ ] **Option A: Discovery-First** — App is a curated product discovery engine (Sephora meets Wirecutter); users browse by outcome, read research, get AI recommendations, buy. Value = curation intelligence.
- [ ] **Option B: Protocol-First** — App delivers a personalized daily wellness protocol with products woven in; commerce is the natural outcome of following the protocol. Value = "your smartest friend managing your health."
- [ ] **Option C: Community-First** — App is a social platform where products surface through community behavior and social proof; Xiaohongshu for longevity. Value = belonging and peer validation.
- [ ] **Hybrid: Protocol-First + Discovery Support** — Protocol is the primary experience (daily engagement, product integration, AI concierge builds your routine). Discovery is the secondary experience (browse/explore for users who want to go beyond their protocol). Community data layers onto both as social proof.

## Sub-Decisions
- [ ] **App's #1 screen after onboarding:** Protocol view (Today's Routine) / Discovery feed / Community feed
- [ ] **Product integration style:** Inline within protocol / Dedicated shop tab / Both (inline primary, shop tab secondary)
- [ ] **AI concierge role:** Product recommender / Protocol builder / Both (protocol builder primary, product recommendations flow from protocol)
- [ ] **Physical space role in new model:** Core pillar / Supporting channel / Phase 2 experience amplifier
- [ ] **The "aha moment" — what should users experience in first 5 minutes?** Personalized protocol generated / Curated product discovery feed / Community welcome + goal matching
- [ ] **Four pillars hierarchy in deck:** Equal weight (current) / Clear hierarchy with Product Commerce as #1 engine
- [ ] **Onboarding depth:** Light (4 screens, fast to value) / Deep (8+ screens, better personalization, higher drop-off risk)
- [ ] **Shop tab existence:** Yes, standalone / No, products only appear in protocol context / Both (protocol inline + browse tab)
- [ ] **Subscribe-and-save timing:** Offered at first purchase / Offered at first reorder / Offered at 30-day checkpoint

## Specialist Recommendation: Hybrid — Protocol-First + Discovery Support

## Rationale
73% of consumers feel overwhelmed buying health products online — they need direction, not another catalog. A protocol-first approach solves the core problem stated in Alche's own deck ("forced to become project managers of their own health") while creating daily engagement that a discovery-only app cannot. The data supports this: AI-guided recommendations drive 369% higher AOV and 4x better conversion, and 50.3% of repeat purchases happen within 30 days — meaning daily engagement in Month 1 is the single highest-leverage factor for revenue. Discovery remains essential as the browse layer for users who want to explore beyond their protocol, but the protocol is the spine that holds everything together.
