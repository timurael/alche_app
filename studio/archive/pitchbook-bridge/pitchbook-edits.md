## SLIDE 2 — The Problem

**Headline:** The Problem

**Body:**
People are more health-conscious than ever — but the longevity space is overwhelming, clinical, and gatekept.

**Bullet points:**
- Information overload — thousands of conflicting protocols, supplements, and biohacks
- Clinical and intimidating — most longevity content is designed for scientists, not everyday people
- Expensive gatekeeping — personalized longevity plans cost $1,000+ through concierge services
- No single source of truth — users bounce between podcasts, Reddit threads, and influencer advice
- Habit fatigue — even motivated users struggle to build consistent, evidence-based routines
