## Graveyard Analysis (Locked: $1.4B+ Total)

| Company | What Happened | Lesson for alche |
|---------|--------------|-----------------|
| Forward Health | Burned $657M, shut down | Asset-heavy model (clinics) unsustainable at scale |
| DayTwo | 2,089 citations, still failed | Scientific rigor alone doesn't create consumer product |
| Arivale | Shut down 2019 | Consumers don't pay for preventing future disease -- sell how you feel today |
| Noom | Revenue declined | Behavioral coaching alone has retention ceiling |

**Total failed investment**: $1.4B+ across health/nutrition startups

**Three survivor patterns** (from graveyard analysis):
1. Asset-light model (no manufacturing, no hardware)
2. Content-led acquisition (build audience before product)
3. Community-first retention (social identity > feature utility)
