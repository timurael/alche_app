# Section-by-Section Transformation Decisions

**Source**: `_INVESTOR_EMPATHY_MAP.md` Section C (Gap Analysis) + Builder Agent execution
**Relevance**: What changed in each section and why

---

## Section 00 — Cover
**Priority**: MEDIUM
**What changed**: "The Art of Curated Longevity" replaced with "Pre-Seed Investment Memorandum." Wordmark reduced from 120px to 56px. Three brand pillars (Taste, Trust, Cultural Authority) replaced with three business pillars (EUR 500K / EUR 2.5M / Longevity Lifestyle Platform). Gradient accent bars removed.
**Why**: Cover must signal "investment document" not "brand catalog" in the first 5 seconds.
**Source**: Empathy Map, Filter 1 ("Is this a business or brand exercise?")

---

## Section 00b — Table of Contents (NEW)
**Priority**: HIGH
**What changed**: Did not exist. Created functional TOC with dot leaders, page numbers, and four section groups.
**Why**: Every consulting pitch book has a TOC on page 2. Its absence is a structural red flag signaling amateur document construction. Investors navigate by TOC to jump to financials or team.
**Source**: Empathy Map, Deloitte style definition + structural gap analysis

---

## Section 00c — Executive Summary (NEW)
**Priority**: CRITICAL
**What changed**: Did not exist. Created 2-page executive summary with KPI strip, 2x2 summary grid, investment thesis table, key risks, team summary, deal structure.
**Why**: The single biggest structural gap. An investor who reads only this page should understand the entire opportunity. Its absence meant the investor had to read 26 pages to assemble the picture themselves.
**Source**: Empathy Map, Filter 1 + consulting document standards

## Section 06 — Product

## Section 07 — Business Model (3 pages)
**Priority**: CRITICAL
**What changed**:
- **Page 1**: Hero stat "145 subscribers to break-even" leads. Break-even path table. NEW sensitivity analysis (base/downside/worst/upside — worst case still within 24-month runway).
- **Page 2**: Unit economics as single table (conservative 5.1x vs realistic 14.7x LTV:CAC). Pricing structure. Gross margin by revenue stream.
- **Page 3**: Conversion funnel as data table (not visual funnel). Revenue composition bars simplified. Graveyard lessons as footnotes.
**Why**: Most critical section for business-heavy investor. Break-even was buried on page 16. No sensitivity analysis existed. Conversion rates were unanchored. All assumptions now visible.
**Source**: Empathy Map Section C (CRITICAL priority) + `_INVESTOR_CRITIQUE.md` Section 07 critique
