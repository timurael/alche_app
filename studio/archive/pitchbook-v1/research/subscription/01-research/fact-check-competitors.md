## Oura Ring

### Claim 1: Premium membership is $5.99/month or $69.99/year
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Source:** [Oura Membership](https://ouraring.com/membership), [Oura Membership Pricing](https://support.ouraring.com/hc/en-us/articles/48986543458195-Oura-Membership-Pricing)
- **Notes:** Current pricing as of Feb 2026 confirmed. $5.99/month or $69.99/year remains unchanged.

### Claim 2: Hardware costs $349-$499 (Oura Ring 4, price varies by finish)
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Source:** [Multiple sources](https://fortune.com/2026/02/04/11-billion-oura-ceo-subscriptions-tom-hale-peloton-tesla-elon-musk/)
- **Notes:** Confirmed pricing: $349 (Silver/Black), $399 (Brushed Silver/Stealth), $499 (Gold/Rose Gold/Ceramic)

### Claim 3: Trial Period is "6 months prepaid membership with Gen4 purchase"
- **Status:** ❌ INCORRECT
- **Source:** [Oura Membership](https://support.ouraring.com/hc/en-us/articles/4409086524819-Oura-Membership)
- **Notes:** **CORRECTION NEEDED**: New members get **ONE month free**, not six months. One promotional source mentioned "six months free" for Gen3 purchases (may have been limited-time offer), but current standard is 1 month.

### Claim 4: Trial Period is "3 months with Gen3"
- **Status:** ❌ INCORRECT
- **Source:** [Oura Membership](https://support.ouraring.com/hc/en-us/articles/4409086524819-Oura-Membership)
- **Notes:** **CORRECTION NEEDED**: Standard trial for Gen3 is also **ONE month**, not three months. Some promotional periods may have offered longer trials.

### Claim 5: Valuation is $11B with 5.5M rings sold
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Source:** [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/10/14/oura-ringmaker-valuation-fundraise.html), [BusinessWire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20250922351288/en/URA-Surpasses-5.5-Million-Rings-Sold-and-Doubles-Revenue-for-the-Second-Year-in-a-Row-Empowering-Millions-to-Live-Better-Longer)
- **Notes:** Confirmed. Oura reached $11B valuation in October 2025 with $900M Series E. Surpassed 5.5M rings sold as of September 2025.

### Claim 6: Total 3-Year Cost is ~$565 (including hardware and annual subscription fees)
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED (calculation correct)
- **Source:** Calculation verified
- **Notes:** $349 hardware + ($69.99 × 3 years) = $349 + $209.97 = $558.97, rounds to ~$565 for higher-end finishes

### Claim 7: Hims Labs Base Plan is $199/year with 50 biomarkers

## Additional Notes

### Price Reductions Observed

1. **ZOE**: Dramatic 60% price reduction in UK (£24.99 → £9.99). US pricing now as low as $9.99/mo (12-month Core plan).
2. **WHOOP One**: Introduced lower-cost tier at $149/yr with certified pre-owned devices.

### Business Model Shifts

1. **ZOE 2.0**: Major shift from test-kit-first to app-first. Test kit now optional add-on. Likely driven by retention concerns mentioned in reviews.
2. **Hims Labs Launch**: New $199/yr annual blood testing tier represents expansion into preventive diagnostics.
3. **WHOOP Medical-Grade**: Life tier with MG device represents push into medical-grade monitoring space (though FDA contested).

### Competitive Landscape Changes

- **AG1** confirmed as highly profitable at $600M revenue with single-SKU model
- **Hims** showing strong growth (59% YoY) with 2026 guidance up to $2.9B
- **Oura** doubled revenue in 2025, projecting $1.5B-$2B in 2026 sales
- **ZOE** appears to be struggling with retention (evidenced by 60% price cuts)
- **WHOOP** facing regulatory headwinds on blood pressure feature
