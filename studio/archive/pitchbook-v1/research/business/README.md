## Key Insights

### 1. The Business Model is Asset-Light, Digital-First
- **Pre-Seed (EUR 500K):** Digital-first, app-focused, zero physical buildout
- **Seed (EUR 3M):** Berlin Alche Salon space launches as ONE revenue module with separate P&L
- **Never own hardware:** We're the software interpretation layer, not the device manufacturer

**Why This Matters:** Avoids "Two-Business Death Spiral" that killed Forward Health ($657M burned).

---

### 2. Unit Economics Are Defensible (13.3x LTV:CAC)
- **LTV (realistic):** EUR 533 (EUR 49 ARPU × 87% margin × 12.5 mo lifetime)
- **CAC (blended):** EUR 40 (content-first EUR 0 CAC + paid EUR 25 + walk-ins EUR 115)
- **LTV:CAC:** 13.3x (well above 3x minimum, 5x "excellent" threshold)

**Why This Matters:** Investors want to see 5x+ LTV:CAC. alche delivers 13x.

---

### 3. Churn is the Model's Most Sensitive Assumption
- **alche target:** 8% monthly churn (defended by community + data compounding)
- **Industry baseline:** 12-15%
- **Impact:** 8% churn = EUR 533 LTV. 15% churn = EUR 285 LTV (46% drop)

**Why This Matters:** If churn exceeds 10%, unit economics deteriorate. Community retention (Alche Salon events, #dailyalche ritual) is not optional — it's structural.

---

### 4. Content-First Acquisition = EUR 0 CAC for 8-10 Months
- **Phase 1 (M-8 to M4):** TikTok, newsletter, YouTube, Alche Salon events = EUR 0 capital spend
- **Phase 2 (M4-M12):** Only spend EUR 50-100K on paid ads AFTER organic validation (500+ waitlist)

**Why This Matters:** Proof of demand before burning capital. AG1 playbook adapted for European longevity market.

---

### 5. Competitive Window is Real (12-18 Months)
- **Oura:** Entering German market 2025, but full localization takes 12-18 months
- **Hims:** ZAVA acquisition still integrating
- **ZOE:** UK-centric, no German plans

**Why This Matters:** First-mover advantage = community moat. By the time Oura fully localizes, alche has 500+ subs, Alche Salon established, #dailyalche ritual in market.

---

### 6. The Graveyard is alche's Competitive Moat
- **$1.4B+ burned** by Forward Health, Arivale, Juicero, Noom, others
- **7 structural traps** alche deliberately avoids (documented in `04-graveyard-lessons.md`)
- **Pattern recognition is defensible:** We know exactly what NOT to do

**Why This Matters:** "Why won't alche fail like Forward/Arivale?" is investor question #1. We have a 7-page answer.
