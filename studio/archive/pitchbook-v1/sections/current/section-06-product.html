What Users See and Do

The app is the operating system. The physical space is the proof of concept. Every interaction feeds a single closed loop.

User Journey

One visit, fully connected.

01
Choose Your Outcome
User selects their primary goal from five outcome categories. This determines protocol templates, product recommendations, and content feed. One tap, one direction. Everything aligns.
MVP
02
Book an LED Session
Real-time seat availability in 20-minute slots. Two types: Glow (red light) and Recovery (near-infrared). During booking, the app suggests a smoothie + boost combo matched to the session.
MVP
03
Customize Your Potion
Six signature Alche Potions (€7-11) with five functional boost add-ons (+€2 each), mapped to outcome categories. Pre-ordered during booking, ready on arrival. Target average ticket: €11.
MVP
V1: Protocol-linked
04
Check In. Session. Track.
QR check-in on arrival. Potion ready, seat ready -- zero wait. Post-session: log how you feel, earn a milestone. This creates the behavioral data that powers personalization in V1.
MVP
05
Shop by Outcome
Own-brand blends, capsules, curated third-party products -- organized by what you want, not by ingredient. The boost you loved in your smoothie? Buy the pouch to take home.
MVP: Catalog
V1: Smart replenishment
06
Community and Events
Wellness events, science talks, researcher sessions. Event RSVP, ticket purchase, member-exclusive access. Every event simultaneously strengthens community and generates revenue.

Product Roadmap

MVP is the skeleton. Intelligence comes later.

MVP -- Months 1-4

The Commerce Loop

Booking, menu, shop, membership, events, basic tracking. Commerce-first. The transaction engine running from day one.

V1 -- Months 5-9

The Intelligence Layer

AI concierge, smart replenishment, glucose-smoothie correlation, curated protocols. KNOW and DO layers get real.

V2 -- Months 10+

The Full Platform

Biomarker dashboard, bio age score, personalized AI protocols, doctor integration. The flywheel at full velocity.

Physical Space = Phase 2
The Berlin flagship in Mitte is where the digital meets the physical: LED sessions, functional smoothies, curated products, community events. It operates near breakeven standalone, generates €8,800-11,700/month in subscriber LTV through walk-in conversions, and creates the environmental cues that bridge the 66-day habit formation gap digital-only tools structurally cannot.
