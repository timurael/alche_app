Product Roadmap

MVP is the skeleton. Intelligence comes later.

MVP: Months 1 to 4

The Commerce Loop

LED booking, digital menu, membership, shop, events, basic progress tracking. Commerce-first: the transaction engine running from day one. Stripe integration, credit-based deductions, real-time inventory.

V1: Months 5 to 9

The Intelligence Layer

Goal-based journeys, Glow Scan, smart replenishment, adaptive check-ins, in-store mode. The data captured in MVP now drives personalization. Protocols become dynamic.

V2: Months 10+

The Full Platform

Digital Twin, AI-driven protocol optimization, advanced progress tracking, community features, wearable sync. The feedback loop at full velocity.

Physical Space = Phase 2
The Berlin flagship in Mitte is where digital meets physical: LED sessions, functional smoothies, curated products, community events. It operates near breakeven standalone, generates €8,800-11,700/month in subscriber LTV through walk-in conversions, and creates the environmental cues that bridge the 66-day habit formation gap digital-only tools structurally cannot.
