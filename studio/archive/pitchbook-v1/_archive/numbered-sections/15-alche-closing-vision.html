Positioning clarity
What Alche is not
Not a wellness brand that happens to have an app
The app IS the business. The physical space is one revenue module -- proof of concept, not the model. Every competitor that led with physical has struggled to scale. We lead with digital and use physical to compound retention.
Not a longevity clinic for the already-converted
We serve people who want to live better without becoming a full-time biohacker. The "imperfect longevity" positioning is not limiting -- it's the entrance. 95% of the market lives here. The committed users find us anyway.
Not another personalized nutrition play
We learned from the graveyard. The test-to-food closed loop killed every venture that attempted it. Alche uses a partial loop: biomarker data informs general protocol recommendations, not prescription meal plans. Simpler ops, better retention.
Not a Silicon Valley export
Built in Berlin. Designed for European culture, European regulation, European sensibility. GDPR-native from day one. German health system integration as a feature, not a workaround. The anti-thesis of optimization culture dressed up as wellness.
