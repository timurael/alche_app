What is alche?

"alche is a premium wellness ecosystem that integrates a physical flagship space, a proprietary mobile app, an outcome-based product brand, and a curated event programme into one data-enabled membership."

Physical Experience
Berlin Mitte Flagship
LED therapy rooms, functional smoothie bar, curated product shelf. The physical anchor that creates habit loops digital tools cannot.
Product Brand
Outcome-Based Blends
Six signature formulations: Glow, Energy, Calm, Recovery, Gut, Reset. Each mapped to a wellness goal, sold as smoothies in-store and as pouches to take home.
App Platform
10 Integrated Modules
Booking, tracking, shopping, community, events, membership, AI concierge, replenishment, biomarkers, and digital twin. One app, one profile.
Events and Culture
Community Programme
Workshops, expert talks, community sessions. Every event builds belonging and generates revenue simultaneously.
Each pillar generates behavioral data that feeds a central information system, enabling personalization across the entire ecosystem. The integration is the product.
