The Product

A physical-digital wellness company operating a flagship space, a proprietary mobile application, an outcome-based product brand, and a membership programme.

alche is a physical-digital wellness company. It operates a flagship space in Berlin Mitte offering LED red light therapy and functional smoothies, a proprietary mobile application with 10 integrated modules, an outcome-based product brand, and a membership programme. Every interaction generates structured data that feeds a central information system.

Physical (Berlin)
  • Flagship location in Berlin Mitte
  • 6 functional smoothies (Alché Potions Nº1-6, EUR 7-11)
  • 5 functional boosts (+EUR 2 each)
  • LED red light therapy (Glow 630-660nm, Recovery 810-850nm)
  • Curated product shelf (own blends + third-party)
  • Wellness events and expert sessions
→ Builds trust, generates behavioral data
Digital (Global)
  • Proprietary mobile application (10 modules)
  • Membership tiers (Core EUR 19/mo, Pro EUR 49/mo, Premium EUR 99/mo)

→ Recurring revenue, borderless scalability

The Customer Experience

How It Works

01
Choose Your Goal
User selects a wellness outcome in the app (Glow, Energy, Calm, Sleep, Recovery). The system generates a personalized protocol based on the selected goal.
02
Book a Session
Real-time LED chair availability in 20-minute slots. Smoothie and boost pre-ordered during booking. Two session types: Glow (630-660nm) and Recovery (810-850nm).
03
Visit the Space
QR check-in on arrival. Smoothie ready, LED mask prepared. Zero wait time. Guided 20-minute session with post-session potion.
04
Post-Session Feedback
Log energy, mood, and skin condition. The system captures structured behavioral data that improves future recommendations.
05
Browse and Buy
Product shelf organized by outcome. Own-brand blends available as take-home pouches. The boost in the smoothie becomes the product on the shelf.
06
Smart Follow-Up
App reminds, recommends products, schedules next visit. The loop repeats with better data each time, improving personalization.
