## 🟢 Potion 01: The Focus Alchemist
**Target:** Cognitive Longevity, Neurogenesis, & Acute Focus
**Philosophy:** A daily driver for deep work. Replaces the crash of a second coffee.
**Sensory Profile:** Earthy matcha, bright yuzu, subtle mint.

### Active Ingredients & Scientific Mapping
1. **Hericium erinaceus (Lion's Mane) Fruiting Body Extract (1500mg)**
   - *Science:* Triggers Nerve Growth Factor (NGF) and improves mild cognitive impairment.
   - *Citation:* DOI: 10.1002/ptr.2634 (Phytotherapy Research).
   - *EFSA Compliance:* 100% Fruiting body used. No mycelium. Fully compliant.
2. **L-Theanine (200mg)**
   - *Science:* Synergizes with baseline caffeine to improve alpha brain waves (calm focus).
3. **Citicoline (CDP-Choline) (250mg)**
   - *Science:* Substrate for acetylcholine synthesis, supporting memory and recall.

### Feasibility & Biomarker Mapping
*   **COGS/Scalability Feasibility:** High. Lion's Mane fruiting body is widely available. L-Theanine and Citicoline are low-cost, high-impact stable powders. Expected formulation COGS: <€0.80 per dose.
*   **Biomarker Target:** 
    *   *Subjective:* Improved daily focus (tracked via Alche app self-reporting).
    *   *Objective/Wearable:* Reduced stress markers (increased daytime HRV on Oura/Apple Health).

## 🔴 Potion 02: The Sunday Reset
**Target:** Systemic Inflammation Reduction & Alcohol Recovery
**Philosophy:** The ultimate antidote to a late Saturday night. Reduces systemic stress so you don't miss Monday.
**Sensory Profile:** Tart cherry, warming ginger, black pepper kick.

### Active Ingredients & Scientific Mapping
1. **Curcumin Extract (500mg) + Piperine (5mg)**
   - *Science:* Down-regulates inflammatory cytokines (TNF-α, IL-6) and CRP. Piperine increases bioavailability by up to 2000%.
   - *Citation:* Systematic meta-analyses on C3 complex efficacy.
2. **Tart Cherry Extract (Prunus cerasus) (480mg)**
   - *Science:* Reduces uric acid, enhances recovery, and lowers CRP.
   - *Citation:* NCT02922920 (ClinicalTrials.gov).
3. **Dihydromyricetin (DHM) (300mg)**
   - *Science:* Enhances alcohol metabolism enzymes (ADH and ALDH), protecting the liver.

### Feasibility & Biomarker Mapping
*   **COGS/Scalability Feasibility:** Medium. Curcumin/Piperine is cheap. High-quality Tart Cherry extract and DHM drive the cost up slightly. Expected formulation COGS: ~€1.10 per dose.
*   **Biomarker Target:** 
    *   *Blood Biomarker:* Reduction in hs-CRP (High-Sensitivity C-Reactive Protein) and lowered ALT/AST liver enzymes in quarterly blood panels.

**Target:** Autophagy & Cellular Repair
**Philosophy:** The longevity cornerstone. Taken before bed to optimize overnight repair cycles.
**Sensory Profile:** Deep berry, lavender, subtle chamomile.

### Active Ingredients & Scientific Mapping
1. **Spermidine-Rich Wheat Germ Extract (Providing exactly 6mg Spermidine)**
   - *Science:* Induces cellular autophagy, stabilizes DNA.
   - *Citation:* DOI: 10.1038/s43587-022-00322-9 (Nature Aging).
   - *EFSA Compliance:* Strictly gated at the 6mg maximum daily intake limit mandated by EU 2020/443.
2. **Trans-Resveratrol (250mg)**
   - *Science:* Activates SIRT1 pathways, mimicking caloric restriction.
3. **Apigenin (50mg)**
   - *Science:* Promotes deep sleep architecture, crucial for cellular repair.

### Feasibility & Biomarker Mapping
*   **COGS/Scalability Feasibility:** Low/Medium. True spermidine extracts (vs synthetics) are expensive. Must balance the other ingredients to maintain margin. Expected formulation COGS: ~€1.50 per dose.
*   **Biomarker Target:**
    *   *Wearable:* Increased Deep Sleep duration and lowered Sleep Latency (Oura/Apple Health).
    *   *Blood Biomarker:* Lowered systemic inflammation over 6 months; ApoB stabilization.
