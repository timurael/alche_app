## Fundraising Implications

### Pre-Seed (Current Round: EUR 500K)
- **Use of funds:** Cafe buildout (22%), app development (20%), operations/salaries (50%), marketing (8%)
- **Milestones for next round:** 200+ subscribers, monthly revenue EUR 15K+, proven unit economics, community retention data
- **Timeline to next raise:** Month 12-15

### Seed (Projected: EUR 1.5-3M at EUR 8-15M valuation)
- **Trigger:** 200+ subscribers, 6+ months of retention data, proven cafe-to-app conversion, ARPU >EUR 45
- **Use of funds:** Second city, team expansion, app v2, marketing scale
- **Note:** European VCs now demanding $300-500K ARR at seed [Data]. At EUR 50 ARPU, that requires 500-830 subscribers. Base case doesn't reach this until Month 20-24. This means either: (1) raise seed earlier at lower ARR with strong retention metrics, (2) extend runway with bridge/revenue, or (3) accept that seed may be 24+ months away.

If Alche reaches EUR 300K ARR by seed round, a 15x multiple = EUR 4.5M valuation. At 25x (Function Health multiple) = EUR 7.5M. The EUR 2.5M cap is conservative and investor-friendly. [Opinion]

## Flags

**RED:**
- The EUR 500K raise provides 24-month runway ONLY in the base case. Conservative case runs out at ~Month 20. Any significant cost overrun (cafe buildout, CTO salary) compresses this further.
- Seed round timing depends on reaching metrics that the base case doesn't hit until Month 20-24. There's a potential 6-12 month gap between "need to raise" and "ready to raise." A bridge round may be necessary.

**YELLOW:**
- ARPU at EUR 50 is at the high end of the healthy benchmark range ($10-50/mo). If ARPU settles at EUR 35-40, the entire financial model shifts by 6-12 months.
- Annual billing at 40% assumption drives better revenue predictability but requires discounting (15%), which reduces effective ARPU to EUR 43-47.
