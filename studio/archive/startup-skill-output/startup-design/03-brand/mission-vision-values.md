# Mission, Vision & Values -- Alche

> **Version:** 1.0 | March 2026

---

## Mission

**Make longevity science accessible, joyful, and communal -- starting with the women who have been excluded from it.**

Alche exists because the longevity revolution was built for a narrow audience: wealthy, male, optimization-obsessed. We believe aging well is a human right, not a biohacking achievement. We bring the science to the table -- literally, around food, in community, through a space you actually want to be in.

---

## Vision

**By 2030, Alche is the European standard for community-anchored longevity -- the brand that proved science and joy are not opposites, and that aging well is better together.**

## Values

### 1. Science with Soul
We don't choose between rigor and warmth. Every recommendation we make is grounded in evidence. And every experience we design is grounded in humanity. We cite our sources and we care about your feelings. Both.

**In practice:**
- Every product/protocol references published evidence
- Every touchpoint is designed with emotional intelligence
- We never use fear to sell
- We admit what we don't know

### 2. Imperfect Progress
Perfection is not the goal. Progress is. We reject the idea that you need to optimize every biomarker, biohack every morning, or track every macro to live well. Do what you can. Celebrate what you do. Start again when you stop.

**In practice:**
- No shame language in any communication
- No "streaks" that punish you for missing a day
- Content celebrates small wins
- Community norms emphasize support over comparison

### 3. Together Over Alone
Health is a team sport. The longest-living people on Earth don't biohack alone in a lab -- they eat together, walk together, care for each other. Community is not a feature; it's the foundation.

**In practice:**
- Physical space is designed for gathering, not consumption
- Events are collaborative, not lecture-format
- App features community before individual dashboards
- Pricing includes community access at every tier

### 4. Women First, Everyone Welcome
Longevity science has ignored women's bodies for decades. We center female physiology, life stages, and experiences. And we welcome anyone who shares our values.

**In practice:**
- Protocols address perimenopause, menopause, hormonal cycles
- Content and imagery represent diverse women
- Medical partnerships include women's health specialists
- Language is inclusive, not exclusionary

### 5. Honest Always
We don't oversell, overclaim, or overpromise. If the science is uncertain, we say so. If a product doesn't have strong evidence, we don't carry it. Trust is our most valuable asset.

**In practice:**
- Evidence tiers clearly labeled on all content
- Supplements/products vetted against published research
- Financial projections use conservative assumptions
- We share failures and learnings publicly

## Anti-Values (What Alche is NOT)

| We are NOT | Why | Example |
|---|---|---|
| An optimization platform | Optimization culture is the problem, not the solution | No leaderboards, no "scores," no gamified streaks |
| A clinic | Clinical = cold, transactional, intimidating | Cafe, not waiting room. Community, not patients. |
| A supplement company | Products support the platform, not the other way around | Never lead with a product; lead with community + science |
| A content brand | Content is a channel, not the business | Revenue from subscription + space, not ads or sponsorships |
| A biohacking brand | Biohacking is exclusionary and trend-dependent | Language avoids "hack," "optimize," "stack" |

## Flags

**YELLOW:**
- "Women First, Everyone Welcome" is a nuanced positioning that requires careful execution. Too women-focused = limits TAM. Too inclusive = loses differentiation. Test messaging with diverse audiences.
- "Imperfect Progress" could be interpreted as "low standards." The messaging must convey ambition + grace, not complacency.

---

*Values are only meaningful if they constrain decision-making. Every product, content, and design decision should be testable against these 5 values.*
