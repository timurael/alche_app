## Decision Checkpoints

### End of Week 2: Mini-Assessment
- Are interviews revealing consistent themes?
- Is concept testing response rate sufficient?
- Any early kill signals?

### End of Week 4: Major Assessment
- Does a winning positioning variant emerge?
- Is WTP at EUR 35+/mo confirmed?
- Did the first event generate excitement and signups?
- Are there CTO candidates worth pursuing?
- Is the Heilpraktiker pathway confirmed?

### If Week 4 assessment is POSITIVE:
Continue to second set of pop-up events (Weeks 5-6). Begin fundraise in earnest. Commission cafe space search. Make CTO offer.

### If Week 4 assessment is NEGATIVE:
Pause. Review kill criteria. Determine which hypothesis failed and whether a pivot (different positioning, different price, different segment) is worth testing.
