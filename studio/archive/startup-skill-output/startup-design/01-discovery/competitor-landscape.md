**Differentiation from Alche:**

**Kill scenario:** ZOE expands into multi-dimensional longevity, opens European physical spaces, targets women. Probability: LOW near-term (18-24 months) given focus on US expansion and nutrition depth. [Opinion]

## The Science Credibility Gap -- Honest Assessment

| Brand | Published Papers | Clinical Evidence | Alche Equivalent |
|---|---|---|---|
| ZOE | 70+ (Nature, Lancet) | Multiple RCTs | None (planned via partnerships) |
| Function Health | N/A (testing platform) | FDA-cleared AI for MRI | None |
| Seed | Multiple clinical trials | Human RCTs | None |
| NOVOS | Human RCTs | Available at Mayo Clinic | None |
| Alche | 0 | 0 | Medical advisor (CGM) + planned partnerships |

**This is the single biggest credibility risk for the investor pitch.** ZOE has 70+ papers. Function has FDA-cleared AI. Seed has clinical trials. Alche has a medical advisor and planned partnerships. The asset-light model is smart operationally but creates a science credibility gap that must be addressed explicitly. Options: (1) Partner with established institutions early, (2) Commission a small study, (3) Build evidence library from partners' published work. [Opinion]
