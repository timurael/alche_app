## Gate Decision: CONDITIONAL GO

**Proceed to Strategy (Phase 4) with the following conditions:**

### Must-Do Before Fundraise:
1. **30 customer interviews** -- non-negotiable. Validate positioning, WTP, audience demographics.
2. **Pricing study** -- Van Westendorp or conjoint. The EUR 49/mo mid-tier is in the most contested band.
3. **One pop-up event** -- Test the food-as-social-object hypothesis with real humans in Berlin.
4. **Heilpraktiker legal review** -- Confirm scope before committing to service offerings.

### Must-Do Before Launch:
1. **Signed lab partnership** -- Biomarker testing cost and logistics confirmed.
2. **CTO identified** (doesn't need to be hired, but candidate pipeline must exist).
3. **Commercial lease scouted** -- Actual Berlin market data on cafe space cost.

## Strengths That Support GO

1. **Market validation is strong.** Function Health ($2.5B), ZOE ($500M+), Neko ($1.8B) prove consumer longevity is a real, funded market.
2. **Timing alignment is exceptional.** GWS 2026 over-optimization backlash + women's longevity breakout = perfect cultural moment.
3. **Positioning gap is real.** No brand in the upper-right quadrant (science + joy) at scale. ZOE is closest but nutrition-only.
4. **Competitor distraction.** Hims in regulatory crisis (SEC/DOJ). ZOE focused on US expansion. Function is US-only. European field is open.
5. **Germany's market depth.** 3rd-largest wellness market globally ($281B). Berlin emerging as European longevity hub.
6. **Asset-light architecture.** Avoids every specific failure mode that killed $800M+ in longevity startups.
7. **Community moat potential.** 23% churn reduction from community features is significant if achieved.

## Risks That Almost Stopped GO

1. **Zero customer validation.** Every customer-facing assumption is untested. The "Imperfect Longevity" positioning may resonate culturally but not commercially.
2. **Science credibility gap.** 0 papers, 0 clinical evidence vs. ZOE's 70+ papers and Function's FDA-cleared AI. This is the #1 investor objection.
3. **Break-even math is knife-edge.** 145 subscribers + EUR 10K/mo space leaves zero buffer. One bad month and cash runs out.
4. **Community in longevity is unproven.** No precedent at scale. Fitness community works because of daily engagement and identity formation -- longevity may not have these properties.
5. **Price band compression.** EUR 49/mo competes with Function ($30/mo), ZOE (~$60/mo), Levels (~$65/mo). The value must clearly exceed these.
6. **European VC selectivity.** Investors now demanding $300-500K ARR at seed. Pre-seed with zero revenue sets a high bar for the next round.

## What Would Change This to NO-GO

1. Customer interviews reveal "Imperfect Longevity" does not convert to WTP above EUR 30/mo.
2. Berlin cafe buildout costs exceed EUR 150K (eating too much of the EUR 500K raise).
3. Heilpraktiker pathway does not cover the intended service offerings.
4. ZOE announces multi-dimensional longevity expansion and European physical spaces.
5. A well-funded European competitor emerges with the same positioning (not yet visible but could be in stealth).
