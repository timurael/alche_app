**Primary Acquisition Channel:** Podcast + SEO

- 3.5M monthly organic audience [Data]
- ZOE Science & Nutrition podcast: top 10 Health globally
- SEO: organic traffic grew 754% in 6 months (to 3.2M monthly visits, June 2024) [Data]

**Partnerships:** Steven Bartlett / Diary of a CEO, supermarket distribution for supplements

**Key Takeaway for Alche:** ZOE proves that a science-credibility-first content strategy can build a $100M+ business. The podcast model is replicable but requires deep scientific authority.
