ICS framework · square 1:1
Image type: 3D character render, botanical still life photography style, square 1:1 frame, centered composition, pale cream-off-white backdrop.

Content: a tiny sprout creature named Filiz. The character is a pale cream-green root-bulb body (~60% of character volume), with two small symmetric (but not mirrored — slightly asymmetric for liveliness) oval leaves extending sideways as arms, and one curled unfurling tendril on top slightly off-center like a cowlick. Height-to-width ratio 1:1.4. Body has a matte surface with faint morning-dew sheen on crown. Leaves show subsurface-scattering — light passes through them revealing a single midvein shadow. Leaf tips gently pointed. Two 5px round black-dot eyes mid-body, with a tiny single eyelash tuft above each (3 delicate strokes each — pure sentiment). Soft curved smile 10px wide. Two pinkish blush ovals at 25% opacity. Slight warm-brown fade at the very bottom of the root-bulb, suggesting the sprout was just lifted from soil.

Style: Pixar-Disney cute with botanical illustration DNA, 3D subsurface scattering on leaves, morning-dew micro-highlights, soft overcast lighting (early-morning window light), shallow depth of field. References: Totoro's soot-sprites and kodama (warm, not eerie), Apple Health rings meet a living houseplant, Muji's garden collection, Studio Ghibli nature-spirits. NO cartoon outline. NO chibi-anime features. NO visible human arms — the leaves ARE the arms.
