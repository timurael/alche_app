## MASTER PROMPT (hero render)

```
Image type: 3D character render, botanical still life photography style, square 1:1 frame, centered composition, pale cream-off-white backdrop.

Content: a tiny sprout creature named Filiz. The character is a pale cream-green root-bulb body (~60% of overall character volume), with two small symmetric (but not mirrored — slightly asymmetric for liveliness) oval leaves extending sideways as arms, and one curled unfurling tendril on top slightly off-center like a cowlick or antenna. Height-to-width ratio 1:1.4 (taller than wide because the leaves extend horizontally). Body has a matte surface with faint morning-dew sheen on the crown. Leaves show subsurface-scattering — light passes through them, revealing a single midvein shadow. Leaf tips are gently pointed. Two 5px round black-dot eyes mid-body, with a tiny single eyelash tuft above each eye (3 delicate strokes each — the only detail that gives pure sentiment). Soft curved smile 10px wide. Two pinkish blush ovals at 25% opacity. Slight warm-brown fade at the very bottom of the root-bulb, suggesting the sprout was just lifted from soil.

Style: Pixar-Disney cute with botanical illustration DNA, 3D subsurface scattering on leaves, morning-dew micro-highlights, soft overcast lighting (like early-morning window light), shallow depth of field. References: Totoro's soot-sprites and kodama (but warm, not eerie), Apple Health rings meet a living houseplant, Muji's garden collection photography, Studio Ghibli nature-spirits, contemporary botanical 3D illustration. NO cartoon outline. NO chibi-anime features. NO visible arms that look like human limbs — the leaves ARE the arms.
```

## VARIATIONS (expression sheet)

### Idle (default chat state)
```
Same character Filiz. Eyes open, small soft smile. Leaves gently resting in a neutral horizontal position. Upright root-bulb body. Sweet and settled.
```

### Listening (user is typing)
```
Same character Filiz. Eyes larger/wider (upper lid raised 2px), mouth a tiny "o" shape. Both leaves lifted slightly upward (angled ~10° up from horizontal), like attentive ears. Body slightly forward.
```

### Thinking (API pending)
```
Same character Filiz. Eyes looking upward-right, mouth gently closed. The tendril on top curling and unfurling (capture mid-motion, one frame of unfurl). Body still. Soft ambient shimmer on crown.
```

### Delighted (user submits)
```
Same character Filiz. Eyes closed into upward happy arcs, wide open smile. Both leaves raised high (~35° up from horizontal) like arms cheering. Tiny bounce pose — body slightly squashed vertically.
```

### Sleeping (end of session)
```
Same character Filiz. Eyes closed as horizontal lines. Smile flat neutral. Both leaves folded gently around the front of the body, as if hugging itself. Tendril flopped to one side. Single "z" floating above, 40% opacity, serif style.
```
