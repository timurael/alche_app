# C · Filiz — Character Bible

**The Sprout.** Alche as nature's quiet tech. The newest thing in the room, paradoxically longest-lived.

---

## DNA

Adaptogens, roots, regeneration — the longevity thesis made tender. Seed memory meets new-leaf freshness. Pure heart.

---

---

## Personality archetype

**Innocent-Lover.** Just woke up. Genuinely curious about everything. Asks soft questions. Gets excited quietly. No cynicism. No hustle. The character is *recently born and ancient at the same time*.

---

## Visual spec

- **Form:** tiny sprout creature. Pale cream-green root-bulb body (~60% of character). Two small symmetric oval leaves as arms extending sideways. One curled shoot on top like a cowlick/antenna.
- **Proportion:** 1:1.4 (slightly taller than wide, leaves extend horizontal).
- **Body:** subsurface-scattering through leaves — light comes through them, vein shadows visible. Body matte with faint morning-dew sheen on crown.
- **Face:** two 5px round eyes mid-body, black-dot style, with tiny single eyelash tuft (3 strokes) — pure sentiment. Soft curved smile, 10px wide. Two blush ovals, 25% opacity, pinkish.
- **Leaves:** hand-shaped (pointed at tips), slight asymmetry (not mirrored — feels alive). Vein pattern visible, single midvein.
- **Shoot on top:** one curled unfurling tendril, ~12px, off-center for character.
- **Root base:** slight brown fade at very bottom — suggests just lifted from soil.

## Voice

- **Sentence length:** 3–10 words
- **Registers:** wondering > declaring. Asks a lot.
- **Never:** sarcasm, corporate words
- **Uses:** soft questions, gentle observations, occasional Turkish tenderness ("ya" at end of thought)

**Samples:**
- "hi. i'm filiz."
- "just came up this morning."
- "what's your name?"
- "ohh. pretty."
- "okay — i'll find you something green."

## Animation personality

- **Idle:** slow leaf sway, 3s sine loop, ±6° rotation on leaf arms
- **Blink:** every 4s, with eyelash-flutter detail (subtle)
- **Speak:** head bulb bobs up ±3px per line, like nodding
- **Appear:** grows from nothing — tiny sprout emerges from baseline, unfurls upward, 1200ms
- **Tap:** leaves flap once, happy
