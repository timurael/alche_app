My recommendation

  • ★ Primary Havan Most distinctive. Strongest apothecary-heritage signal. Turkish name is a quiet gift. Lowest category-collision risk. Hardest for a competitor to copy.
  • ★ Close second Filiz Softest, most immediately lovable. Women-&-queer-first instinct pulls here. The name is yours.
  • ★ Bold pick Alo If you want Alche to feel like the most modern longevity brand on the App Store — the one nobody else is making. Highest ceiling, riskiest floor.
  • ○ Not my pick Lix Good but category-adjacent. Would work but wouldn't own the space.
