Imperfect Longevity
“We believe in the science. We also believe in the second glass of wine.”
Alche Founding Principle — No. 01

A different kind of longevity.

The longevity industry sells two things: clinical optimization for the obsessed, or shallow wellness for the overwhelmed. Neither works for people who want to live well and live long — without turning their body into a project.

alche sits in the space between. We translate peer-reviewed science into daily rituals that feel like pleasure, not prescription. Coffee with functional adaptogens. Movement that builds community. An app that learns your rhythms, not the other way around.

The System

Six pillars,
one practice.

01 — Space

Physical Space

A cafe and apothecary in Berlin. Functional beverages, curated products, and a place where the community gathers. The anchor for everything.

02 — Products

Curated Products

Longevity-aligned supplements, functional foods, and apothecary goods. Tested, sourced, and explained — never just sold.

03 — Intelligence

AI-Powered App

Personalised longevity guidance that adapts to your life. Three tiers from daily rituals to full biomarker integration. Launching 2026.

04 — Access

Membership

Essentials, Integrated, or Complete. A tiered system that grows with your practice — from ritual guidance to lab-partnered health intelligence.

05 — People

Community

Cohort-based learning, practitioner events, and a private network. Longevity is social — the research is clear, and so is our design.

06 — Data

Integrated Information

Wearable data, lab results, and lifestyle inputs — unified. One system that connects what you do, what you consume, and how you feel.

Origin

Rooted in Berlin.

alche launches here because the infrastructure, the community, and the ethos already exist. We’re building on what Berlin already is.
