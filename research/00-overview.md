## The working hypothesis

> No existing longevity brand combines rigorous, multi-dimensional science with human, inclusive, joyful brand positioning -- especially in Europe, especially centering women.

**Confidence level: MODERATE.** The gap is real but narrower than our initial framing suggests. Several brands (ZOE, Seed, Noom) occupy adjacent territory. The honest version of the claim requires qualification. See the full dialectical analysis in `01-positioning-analysis.md`.
