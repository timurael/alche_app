# Insight: European Longevity Landscape -- Who's Building What

## The Finding

No European company currently combines physical space + digital app + biomarker testing + community + lifestyle content at an accessible price point. Several are approaching from different angles. The window is open but closing.

## Tier 1: Direct Threats

| Company | HQ | Raised | What They Do | Threat |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Zoi** | Paris | EUR 20M | Physical center + app + 200+ biomarkers + daily recs. Backed by Xavier Niel, Moderna CEO. | HIGH -- closest to Alche's model, but EUR 3K+/yr and community-weak |
| **Loovi** | Stockholm | EUR 1M | Claims to be *"Europe's first end-to-end digital platform for longevity"* | MEDIUM-HIGH -- same thesis, digital-only, very early |
| **Holo** | Barcelona | EUR 1M | 110+ biomarkers + wearable integration + AI. "Revolut of health" at EUR 199. Ex-Factorial team. | MEDIUM -- right price, strong tech, no physical presence |
| **CARE** | Switzerland | EUR 5.6M | 100+ biomarkers in 20 min, 4K-6K+ members, 4.7 rating across 9 Swiss cities | MEDIUM-HIGH -- real member traction, missing lifestyle layer |

## Tier 2: Berlin Specifically

| Company | What They Do | Relationship to Alche |
|---|---|---|
| **YEARS** | Premium longevity clinic, Ku'damm. $3K-16K+. Apollo Health Ventures-backed. "Clinic-as-a-study" with biobank. | Potential **partner**, not competitor. Different price tier. |
| **Aware** | Blood testing, 100+ biomarkers. EUR 13.6M from Lakestar, Cherry Ventures. | Same city, biomarker overlap. No community, space, or food. |
| **ANTI Berlin** | Longevity + culture venue, Brunnenstrasse. 20K Instagram. | Proves demand for science-meets-lifestyle in Berlin. |
| **Recover Society** | Expanding to Berlin. EUR 500/mo recovery membership in luxury hotels. | Physical wellness but clinical, not lifestyle/community. |

## Tier 3: Luxury Incumbents (Could Move Downstream)

| Company | Capital | Digital Signal |
|---|---|---|
| **Lanserhof** (Austria) | EUR 95M fresh investment | Planning supplements + cosmetics. No consumer app announced. DNA is luxury hospitality. |
| **Clinique La Prairie** (Switzerland) | EUR 300M longevity fund | Brain Health App. Supplements at Bergdorf Goodman. "Longevity Hubs" expanding globally. NYC 2028. |
| **THE WELL** (NYC → Geneva 2026-2027) | Undisclosed | Members-only wellness club. 30K sq ft Geneva. $355+/mo. Strong community, no science. |

## Tier 4: The Big Money to Watch

| Entity | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| **CLP Longevity Fund** (EUR 300M target) | First European fund dedicated to longevity innovation. They are seeing deal flow we're not. Could invest in us *or* in competitors. |
| **Neko Health** ($325M+ raised, $1.8B valuation) | Daniel Ek (Spotify). AI body scans. 100K waitlist, 80% rebook rate. Different model but massive European credibility and investor attention. |
| **Calm/Storm Ventures** (Vienna, EUR 30M) | Backed Holo. Active in European healthtech pre-seed/seed. |
| **Cherry Ventures** (Berlin) | Backed Aware. $500M Fund V. Berlin-native VC. |
| **naturalX Health Ventures** (Berlin, EUR 100M) | Series A healthcare fund based in Berlin. |

## What We Don't Know (Dangerous)

- Stealth pre-seed European startups not yet public
- CLP Longevity Fund portfolio companies
- What European health-tech VCs are seeing in their pipelines
- Whether Neko Health adds lifestyle/community layers to its scanning platform
- Whether THE WELL's Geneva opening succeeds and triggers London/Berlin expansion
