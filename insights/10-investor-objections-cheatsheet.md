# Insight: Investor Objection Cheatsheet

## Quick-reference answers for the 10 most likely investor pushbacks.

---

### "You have no competitors"
**Never say this.** The honest frame: "We have competitors in every dimension. Nobody competes across all dimensions simultaneously. That's the gap."

---

### "What about ZOE?"
ZOE proves science + warmth works commercially ($250M valuation, 100K+ members). They do ~70% of what we claim, but only for nutrition/gut health. No physical space. No multi-dimensional longevity. UK-based. Founder-dependent (Tim Spector). If ZOE expands into broad longevity, they become our primary competitor. Until then, they validate the brand thesis without occupying the full position.

Source: [Ragged Edge / ZOE](https://raggededge.com/work/zoe/)

---

### "Companies that tried this are all dead"
$800M+ in failed longevity startups (Forward, Modern Age, Habit, DayTwo, Ever/Body) all made specific, avoidable mistakes: owning the full value chain, building custom hardware, running expensive clinical operations. Our architecture partners instead of building. The cafe generates revenue; it's not a cost center. Lab partnerships are variable cost, not fixed. We learned from every failure in this graveyard.

Source: [Forbes/GWS](https://www.globalwellnesssummit.com/blog/why-vc-backed-longevity-startups-are-dying-in-a-5-trillion-wellness-market/)

---

### "Your TAM is inflated"
We lead with the longevity-specific market: $23-32B globally at 8-10% CAGR. The $6.8T wellness figure is directional context only. Our SAM is the European women's longevity and wellness segment. Our SOM is Berlin-based, wellness-engaged women 30-55 who currently spend EUR 50+/mo on wellness products and services.

Sources: [Mordor Intelligence](https://www.mordorintelligence.com/industry-reports/longevity-market); [Market Research Future](https://www.marketresearchfuture.com/reports/longevity-market-42067)

---

### "Community is unproven in longevity"
Correct. That's what this round tests. Our hypothesis: food -- not biomarkers -- is the social object that makes longevity community viable. Here's our 6-month test plan: pop-up cafe events, measure sharing behavior, prototype cafe-to-app conversion, track cohort retention. If the community hypothesis fails, we pivot to product-first (supplements/app-led, cafe as marketing only).

---

### "Why can't Restore/Equinox/Hims just add this?"
Restore: 209 franchises = fragmented data, no health data science team, business model rewards treatment frequency not outcomes. Equinox Optimize: $40K/yr, 12 locations, luxury DNA prevents accessibility. Hims: distribution platform, not a brand; 65% stock crash shows regulatory fragility; no community or physical space.

Sources: [SharpSheets](https://sharpsheets.io/blog/restore-hyper-wellness-franchise-costs-profits/); [Hims IR](https://investors.hims.com/)

---

### "How do you compete with Apple Health?"
Apple tells you what happened. We help you understand what it means, do something about it, and surround you with community that keeps you doing it. Apple won't integrate bloodwork (liability), build physical spaces, create community, or frame health as longevity. Apple Health+ (rumored 2026) is the biggest threat, but it will be individual-centric -- no community, no physical touch.

Source: [Apple Insider](https://appleinsider.com/articles/25/10/10/health-could-debut-in-2026-as-apples-next-subscription-service)

---

### "GLP-1 drugs could make this irrelevant"
DayTwo had a 2,089-citation paper and $85M. GLP-1 drugs killed it overnight. Science alone doesn't survive disruption. Community, identity, and daily engagement are the assets that survive. We're building those. Also: GLP-1s address weight, not multi-dimensional longevity (sleep, stress, biomarkers, movement, social connection).

---

### "Why Berlin?"
ANTI Berlin proves demand for science-meets-lifestyle in the city. Berlin Biohackers meetup: 2,213 members. Two major longevity summits in 2026 (April + May). Lower operational costs than London/Paris. Emerging European longevity hub with Aware, YEARS, and growing ecosystem. Regulatory bridge available via Heilpraktiker licensing.

Sources: [The Berliner](https://www.the-berliner.com/berlin/longevity-hype-in-berlin-do-you-want-to-live-forever/); [LIFE Summit Berlin](https://www.lifesummit.berlin/)

---

### "Is this a bad market or bad execution by predecessors?"
Execution failures, not market failures. Evidence: $8.5B in longevity VC funding in 2024 alone. Every failure made identifiable mistakes (vertical integration, custom hardware, no community). The survivors (ZOE, Seed, Bevel) chose asset-light, focused, community-adjacent models. Our architecture is designed on their principles, not the failures'.

Sources: [Crunchbase](https://news.crunchbase.com/venture/longevity-startup-funding-2025-newlimit-data/)
