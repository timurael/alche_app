# Insight: Market Numbers -- What's Real vs. What's Inflated

## The Two Numbers

| Metric | Value | Use in deck |
|---|---|---|
| Global wellness economy | $6.8T (2024), $9.8T by 2029 | Context only -- this includes gyms, spas, tourism, real estate |
| Longevity-specific market | $23-32B (2025-2026), $46-63B by 2031-2035 | **Lead with this** -- it's 350x smaller but actually relevant |

**Insight:** The $6.8T figure is technically accurate but misleading for a longevity platform. Investors who understand TAM/SAM/SOM will flag this immediately. The honest addressable market is the longevity-specific number at 8-10% CAGR.
