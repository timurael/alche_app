## Data Integrity Rules

1. **Never substitute your own data.** If a number is in this document, it has been fact-checked across 35+ research sessions.
2. **If a number seems outdated,** flag it for re-validation with source, don't replace it.
3. **Cite sources in pitch materials.** Investors will ask "where did you get this number?"
4. **"Locked" means locked.** GWI 2024, SpringerMedizin, BEUC, GfK are real sources. Do not invent numbers.
5. **DO NOT USE numbers marked with "DO NOT USE."** They are common mistakes from earlier drafts.
