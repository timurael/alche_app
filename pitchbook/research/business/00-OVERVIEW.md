## What I Built

**Interactive Math Lab** (`/alche-math-lab.html`)
A "Barbie home design meets TI-84 calculator" web tool that makes every aspect of alche's business model tangible and explorable.

### 7 Interactive Modules

1. **Revenue Mixer** — Drag subscriber counts & pricing to see real-time MRR, ARR, ARPU, blended margins
2. **Conversion Funnel** — Visualize how 2,000 free users become 145 paying subscribers
3. **Unit Economics Lab** — LTV, CAC, payback period calculator with live verdicts
4. **Churn Machine** — Watch cohorts decay over 12 months, compare 8% vs 15% scenarios
5. **Break-Even Simulator** — Cash runway visualization showing path to Month 12 profitability
6. **The Graveyard** — $1.4B+ in failed startups and how alche avoids each trap
7. **Glossary** — 12 flip cards explaining every investor term (MRR, ARR, LTV, CAC, etc.)

## Design Language (Applied to Math Lab)

- **Graph paper background** — nerdy-cute, approachable math aesthetic
- **Pink/lavender/mint/peach palette** — warm, playful, not corporate
- **Space Mono for all numbers** — monospace = data precision
- **Cormorant Garamond headlines** — editorial elegance
- **Dark formula boxes** — syntax-highlighted equations like a code editor
- **Glassmorphic cards** — depth, layering, Apple-inspired refinement

**Anti-convergence principle applied:** No Inter fonts, no purple gradients, no generic SaaS patterns. This looks uniquely like alche.
