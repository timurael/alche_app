## Strategy Overview

**Model:** Content-First Flywheel → EUR 0 CAC Phase → Amplify What Converts
**Timeline:** 8-10 months content + community before launch (M4), continue through M12
**Capital Allocation:** EUR 50-100K (15-20% of EUR 500K raise) to paid acquisition ONLY after organic validation

**Why This Works:**
1. **Proof of demand** before spending on ads
2. **Proof of voice** (content format is differentiated and shareable)
3. **Proof of community** (audience already knows each other, trusts alche)
4. **Virality mechanics** built-in (shareable format + identity signal)

## Phase 1: EUR 0 Capital CAC (Pre-Launch, M-8 to M4)

### Primary Channels (Running Now)

#### 1. Short-Form Video (TikTok + Instagram Reels)
**Handle:** @alche.space
**Frequency:** 3-5 posts per week
**Format:** "Honest science that shows what actually works vs. hype"

**Content Pillars:**
- **Industry-Funded vs. Independent Research:**
  - "Collagen supplements: What 23 peer-reviewed studies ACTUALLY say"
  - "Why 90% of longevity influencers are selling you stuff that doesn't work"
  - Visual: Split-screen (industry claim vs. actual study results)

- **Pattern-Breaking Truths:**
  - "You don't need a $4,000 sauna. Here's what actually improves HRV."
  - "Red light therapy: $600 device vs. $30 bulb. The data says…"
  - Tone: Anti-hype, pro-evidence, slightly contrarian

- **Biomarker Deep-Dives:**
  - "What your Oura readiness score ISN'T telling you"
  - "HRV is useless without context. Here's what actually matters."
  - Format: 60-90 sec explainer, no jargon, visual aids

**Target Metrics (Q1-Q2):**
- Followers: 1-2K (Q1) → 3-5K (Q2)
- Engagement rate: 5-8% (industry avg 3-5%)
- Conversion to newsletter: 3-5% of engaged followers

**Why This Works:**
- Shareable: "Did you know…" format drives virality
- Identity signal: Sharing = "I care about aging well, but I'm not obsessed"
- Trust-building: Anti-hype positioning differentiates from wellness influencers

**Source:** agents_prompt.md "Primary Channels"

---

#### 2. Newsletter (Substack/Beehiiv)
**Name:** The Alche Letter (or similar)
**Frequency:** Weekly deep-dive
**Format:** 1,200-1,800 words, evidence-based, no sponsored content

**Content Arcs:**
- **Longevity Protocol Breakdowns:**
  - "I tracked my HRV for 90 days. Here's what actually moved the needle."
  - "The sleep protocol no one talks about (because it's not sexy)"
  - Format: Personal experiment + data + protocol you can copy

- **Honest Product Reviews:**
  - "I spent EUR 2,000 on recovery tools. Here's what's worth it."
  - "CGM for non-diabetics: Is it worth EUR 150/month?"
  - Tone: Brutally honest, no affiliate links initially (builds trust)

- **Science Translation:**
  - "What does 'biological age' actually mean? (Hint: it's not what ZOE sells)"
  - "Microbiome testing: Science vs. marketing"
  - Format: Translate dense research into actionable insights

**Target Metrics:**
- Subscribers: 300 (Q1) → 1,000 (Q2) → 2,500 (Q3) → 5,000 (Q4)
- Open rate: 40-50% (industry avg 20-30%)
- Click-through rate: 8-12%
- Conversion to waitlist: 15-20% of engaged subscribers

**Why This Works:**
- Owned asset: Email list is yours (not rented from Instagram/TikTok)
- Long-form trust: 1,500 words = demonstrates expertise
- Conversion funnel: Newsletter → waitlist → paying subscriber

**Source:** agents_prompt.md "Primary Channels"

### Amplifier Channels

#### 3. YouTube (Monthly "Alche Reviewed" Long-Form)
**Frequency:** 1 video per month (10-20 minutes)
**Format:** Deep-dive product/protocol reviews

**Content Examples:**
- "I wore an Oura Ring for 6 months. Here's what it WON'T tell you."
- "EUR 600 red light panel vs. EUR 30 bulb: Which works better? (I measured HRV)"
- "CGM experiment: 30 days of glucose tracking as a non-diabetic"

**Why Monthly, Not Weekly:**
- YouTube = SEO moat (videos rank for years)
- Quality > quantity (10min video = 3-4 weeks of short-form content)
- Evergreen: One video compounds indefinitely

**Target Metrics:**
- Subscribers: 200 (Q2) → 500 (Q3) → 1,000 (Q4)
- Views per video: 1,000-3,000 (first 30 days)
- Conversion to newsletter: 5-8% of viewers

**Ticket Price:** EUR 50-80 per attendee
**Capacity:** 25-40 people per event
**Frequency:** Quarterly (Q1-Q2) → Bi-monthly (Q3) → Monthly (Q4)

- **Community identity:** #alcheberlin hashtag, user-generated content
- **Acquisition:** ~15% of attendees convert to paid within 30 days (physical touchpoint = trust)

**Leverage Existing Community:**

## Why Content-First Works (Per Founders)

### 1. Proof of Demand
**Before spending EUR 1 on ads:**
- 8-10 months of content engagement
- 500+ waitlist signups
- 5K+ newsletter subscribers
- 250+ event attendees

**Result:** Investors see demand BEFORE asking for capital for paid acquisition.

---

### 2. Proof of Voice
**The "honest science" format is differentiated:**
- Industry-funded vs. independent research contrasts
- Anti-hype positioning (rare in wellness influencer space)
- Shareable format ("Did you know collagen studies are mostly bogus?")

**Result:** Content spreads organically (EUR 0 CAC) because it's contrarian and useful.

---

### 3. Proof of Community
**Audience already knows each other:**
- Alche Salon events = physical meetups
- #dailyalche hashtag = ritual participation
- User-generated content = identity signal ("I'm part of alche Berlin")

**Result:** Community retention (8% churn target vs. 15% industry) is defensible BEFORE launch.

---

### 4. Virality Mechanics
**Why content spreads:**
1. **Shareable format:** "Industry claim vs. actual study results" = pattern interruption
2. **Identity signal:** Sharing alche content = "I care about aging well, but I'm not obsessed"
3. **Ritual participation:** #dailyalche tag creates network effects

**Result:** Each post compounds (content from M1 still drives signups at M12).
