# R10: Retail Product Curation -- Wholesale Brand Research

**Decision Link:** D11 (Retail Product Curation)
**Research Date:** 2026-02-23
**Status:** COMPLETE -- Ready for supplier outreach

---

## Executive Summary

This research identifies 20+ longevity/wellness brands across 5 product categories suitable for curation in alche's Berlin physical space. Key finding: **the P&L assumption of EUR 49 average retail price and EUR 11.50 COGS is partially validated but needs adjustment.** The EUR 49 average retail price is achievable with a curated premium mix, but the EUR 11.50 COGS (implying a 76.5% gross margin) is aggressive. A more realistic COGS range is EUR 15--25 per unit (implying 49--69% gross margins), which is still strong for curated wellness retail.

**Critical regulatory note:** NMN is NOT YET approved as an EU Novel Food. Selling NMN in Germany carries regulatory risk until formal EFSA authorization (expected 2026--2027). All other product categories are fully compliant.

---

## Industry Context: Margin Structure

| Metric | Range | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Supplement gross margins (retail) | 20--50% | Industry standard |
| Premium/curated wellness retail gross margin | 40--60% | Curated store benchmark |
| Typical wholesale discount off retail | 40--60% | B2B standard |
| Keystone markup (retail over wholesale) | 100% (2x) | Standard retail markup |
| Recommended minimum markup | 20% | Competitive floor |
| DTC brand contribution margins | 30--40% | eCommerce benchmark |
| German body care retail gross margin | ~45% | Statista (2020) |

**For alche's curated positioning:** Target 50--60% gross margin (i.e., wholesale cost = 40--50% of retail price). This is achievable with premium brands that offer standard wholesale terms.

### EU Novel Food Status for Mushroom Species

| Species | Fruiting Body | Mycelium | Extract (Supplement) |
|---------|--------------|----------|---------------------|
| Lion's Mane (*Hericium erinaceus*) | NOT Novel | NOVEL (requires authorization) | NOT Novel (aqueous extract) |
| Reishi (*Ganoderma lucidum*) | NOT Novel | NOVEL | NOT Novel |
| Chaga (*Inonotus obliquus*) | NOT Novel | NOVEL | NOT Novel (in supplements only) |
| Cordyceps (*C. sinensis*) | NOT Novel | AUTHORIZED (one of only two approved mycelia) | NOT Novel |
| Shiitake (*Lentinula edodes*) | NOT Novel | AUTHORIZED | NOT Novel |

**Key Takeaway:** Stick to fruiting body-based products and extracts. Avoid mycelium-only products (except Cordyceps sinensis and Shiitake mycelia, which are authorized).

## EU REGULATORY CHECKLIST: Selling Supplements in Berlin

### Mandatory Steps Before Selling

| Step | Requirement | Authority | Timeline |
|------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Register each supplement product with BVL (Federal Office of Consumer Protection and Food Safety) | BVL online portal | Before first sale |
| 2 | Verify all ingredients comply with EU Directive 2002/46/EC and LFGB | Self-assessment | Before ordering |
| 3 | Verify NO Novel Food ingredients without EU authorization (critical for NMN) | EU Novel Food Catalogue | Before ordering |
| 4 | Ensure German-language labeling per EU Regulation 1169/2011 and NemV | Supplier responsibility | Before display |
| 5 | Include mandatory label elements: product name + "Nahrungserganzungsmittel," ingredients list, recommended daily intake, warnings, manufacturer details, batch number, expiry date, storage instructions | Supplier/self | Before display |
| 6 | Verify no disease-related claims on packaging or in-store signage | EC Regulation 1924/2006 | Ongoing |
| 7 | Comply with food contact material regulations for any opened/repackaged products | EU Reg 1935/2004 | If applicable |
| 8 | Maintain product safety records and traceability documentation | Internal | Ongoing |
| 9 | Prepare for random inspections by Berlin state food authorities (Lebensmittelaufsicht) | Berlin Senat | Random |

### Penalties for Non-Compliance
- Failure to register with BVL: Fines up to EUR 50,000
- Incorrect labeling: Fines up to EUR 50,000
- Selling unauthorized Novel Foods: Product recall + fines + reputational damage

### Key Regulations Summary
- **LFGB** (Lebensmittel- und Futtermittelgesetzbuch) -- German Food & Feed Code
- **NemV** (Nahrungserganzungsmittelverordnung) -- German Dietary Supplement Ordinance
- **EU Directive 2002/46/EC** -- Food supplements directive
- **EU Regulation 2015/2283** -- Novel Food regulation
- **EU Regulation 1169/2011** -- Food information to consumers
