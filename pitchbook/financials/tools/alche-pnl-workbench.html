Section 04

P&L Totals

Gross Profit
Total Revenue minus Cost of Goods Sold. This measures how efficiently you deliver your products and services. Target: positive gross margin from M4 onward as revenue lines activate.
Notes
Total Expenses (COGS + OpEx)
All costs combined: direct costs of delivering services (COGS) plus operating overhead (OpEx). With EUR 500K pre-seed, your monthly burn rate determines your runway. At current model: ~EUR 15-22K/mo burn in Y1.
Notes
Net P&L (Bottom Line)
Revenue minus all costs. Break-even target: Month 12, approximately 145 paying subscribers plus EUR 10K/month from the physical space. Pre-seed funding of EUR 500K at EUR 2.5M valuation cap covers the gap until break-even.
Notes
