# THE PHYSICAL ECONOMIST (Sub-Agent 3)
**Focus:** Physical COGS, Wholesale Margins, Shipping, and Packaging in Germany.

## 1. Wholesale Margins for Premium Supplements
- **Benchmark:** The broad dietary supplement industry operates on 30-50% gross margins. For D2C brands, margins can reach 50-70%. When acting as a retailer (buying wholesale to sell retail), the standard brand-to-retailer discount is usually **40% to 50% off MSRP**.
- **The "Alche" Reality:** Alche curates premium, third-party functional foods and supplements rather than manufacturing its own. Because Alche provides high-intent, targeted distribution to a captive audience, negotiating a 50% discount on MSRP is standard for premium wellness brands seeking DACH expansion.
- **Recommended Baseline for P&L:** **50% to 52%** Wholesale Cost (meaning a 48% to 50% gross margin on physical product sales before shipping/packaging).

## 2. Business Shipping Rates (Germany)
- **DHL / Hermes Benchmarks (2024-2025):** 
  - Standard C2C shipping for a small parcel ("Päckchen") is around EUR 3.70 to EUR 4.50. 
  - For B2B/E-commerce with low initial volume (under 500 parcels/year), the new "DHL Kleinpaket" (replacing Warenpost in 2025) starts around **EUR 3.19** net for business customers. 
  - Hermes "Shop-to-Shop" or business spar-sets also hover in the EUR 3.50 - EUR 4.00 range.
- **The "Alche" Reality:** Supplements and functional foods are generally light but require careful tracking. 
- **Recommended Baseline for P&L:** **EUR 3.50 to EUR 4.00 per unit**. (Conservative estimate assumes some home deliveries and peak-season surcharges).

## 3. Premium E-Commerce Packaging
- **Benchmark:** Basic corrugated boxes cost EUR 0.40 - EUR 0.80. However, "unboxing experience" packaging (custom printed mailers, glassine bags, branded tissue paper, insert cards) usually costs between **EUR 1.50 and EUR 2.50** per unit for low-volume orders (e.g., via platforms like Packhelp).
- **The "Alche" Reality:** Alche is a premium, "Apple Glassmorphic x Neo-Apothecary" brand. Standard brown boxes are unacceptable.
- **Recommended Baseline for P&L:** **EUR 1.50 to EUR 2.00 per unit**.
