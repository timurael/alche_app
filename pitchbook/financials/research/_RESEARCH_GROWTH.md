# THE GROWTH ARCHITECT (Sub-Agent 1)
**Focus:** Revenue Metrics, Funnel Conversion, and Marketing OPEX in the Europe/DACH Region.

## 1. Freemium to Paid Conversion Rates
- **Benchmark:** The industry average for general freemium apps is 2-5%.
- **Health & Wellness Specific:** Health and fitness apps tend to perform slightly better, typically converting at **4% to 12%** from download to paid (Adapty.io). 
- **The "Alche" Reality:** Given Alche's model relies heavily on a high-value content hook before asking for the EUR 19/mo Core tier, assuming the top end of standard SaaS (5%) or the lower end of the specialized health app bracket (4-6%) is realistic.
- **Recommended Baseline for P&L:** **4% - 5%** conversion from free user to Core subscriber.

## 2. Monthly Churn Rates (First 90 Days vs. Post-90 Days)
- **Benchmark (First 90 Days):** Health and wellness apps experience brutal initial churn. The 30-day retention averages ~3-12% (meaning 88-97% churn). By Day 90, average retention is roughly 24% to 30%, which means a staggering **70% to 80% total churn** within the first three months.
- **Benchmark (Post-90 Days):** Once users survive the first 90 days (the "habit formation" window), retention stabilizes significantly. Post-90 day monthly churn drops, with annual subscriptions showing much higher retention (33% annual retention vs 17% for month-to-month).
- **The "Alche" Reality:** The locked data highlights that "77% of health app users churn by Day 3." Alche combats this via the IRL community and physical touchpoints. 
- **Recommended Baseline for P&L:** Model a blended **8% monthly churn**, acknowledging that Month 1-2 churn will actually be ~15-20%, stabilizing to ~4-5% for users who stay past Month 3.

## 3. Blended CAC (DACH Region / Organic GTM)
- **Benchmark:** General mobile app Registered User acquisition ranges from $2.17 to $7.55 via paid channels. However, blended CAC (Total Spend / Total Customers) for a premium D2C/subscription model is much higher. 
- **Organic Impact:** Companies leveraging organic growth (SEO, TikTok, community) see significantly lower CAC. For instance, strong organic profiles can drag a $140 paid CAC down to a $77 blended CAC.
- **The "Alche" Reality:** Alche targets a heavily organic, content-first flywheel in Berlin. 
- **Recommended Baseline for P&L:** **EUR 40 - EUR 80**. At a EUR 49 ARPU, an EUR 80 CAC yields a 1.6-month payback period, which is exceptional for pre-seed.

## 4. Marketing OPEX (Micro-Influencer & PR in Germany)
- **Micro-Influencers (Berlin/DACH):** Nano/Micro-influencers (10k - 50k followers) in the wellness space in Germany typically charge **EUR 150 to EUR 500** per dedicated post/reel, often augmented with free product access.
- **PR Retainers (Berlin):** Small boutique tech/lifestyle PR agencies in Berlin typically charge retainers starting around **EUR 2,500 - EUR 4,000/month**. 
- **The "Alche" Reality:** At pre-seed, Alche should NOT hire a PR agency. Co-founder Daria has a media background. Influencer budgets should be focused on product-seeding rather than paid placement.
- **Recommended Baseline for P&L:** **EUR 200 - EUR 400/month** strictly for influencer product gifting and content creation tools. PR retainer: **EUR 0**.
