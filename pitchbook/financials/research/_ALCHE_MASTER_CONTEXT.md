## 1. THE BUSINESS IDENTITY
- **Name:** alche (The Art of Curated Longevity)
- **Location:** Berlin, Germany (All legal, salary, rent, tax, and shipping assumptions must be Germany/Berlin specific).
- **Category:** Longevity Lifestyle Platform (Consumer Wellness, *not* regulated medical/DiGA).
- **The Core Insight:** 80% of health-conscious people don't want an extreme, restrictive protocol (like Bryan Johnson). They are "imperfect longevity seekers." They want the data and the lifestyle, but they still want to drink wine on Friday and eat pasta on Sunday. 

## 2. THE BUSINESS MODEL
Alche is a hybrid model. It is primarily a high-margin digital SaaS business, supported by curated physical products and real-world services. This is designed to create a "sticky" ecosystem. 

There are **4 Revenue Streams:**
1. **App Subscriptions (Core Business - ~68% of Revenue long term):**
   - **Free Tier:** Content and basic community. Used as the top-of-funnel acquisition channel.
   - **Core (EUR 19/mo):** Biomarker dashboard, wearable sync (BYOD - Bring Your Own Device), basic protocols.
   - **Pro (EUR 49/mo):** Personalized protocols, AI-driven insights, advanced tracking.
   - **Premium (EUR 99/mo):** Includes 1:1 reviews, blood panel credits, exclusive events.
2. **Curated E-Commerce Products (Physical):**
   - We do *not* manufacture products (avoids "Closed Loop Trap"). We curate and resell third-party supplements, tools, and functional foods. (Target margin: 40-55%). Avg retail price: EUR 35. 
3. **Wellness Services:**
   - Real-world services provided by freelancers/contractors: LED Therapy sessions (EUR 45), Blood Panel Facilitations (EUR 120), Events/Workshops (EUR 35).
4. **Physical Space (Phase 2 - EUR 0 at Pre-Seed):**
   - A Berlin lounge/bar. For pre-seed modeling, space revenue and space COGS are EUR 0, but we do pay rent/co-working for the founders.

## 3. GO-TO-MARKET (GTM) STRATEGY
- **Content-First Flywheel:** We build an audience *before* we build the heavy tech. Marketing is heavily organic (podcasts, social media, newsletter) to keep CAC low. Paid performance marketing will be used but is not the sole driver.
- **Asset Light:** We integrate with existing wearables (Oura, Apple Health) instead of building hardware. We use existing labs for blood tests. 

## 4. TEAM & FUNDING
- **Funding Ask:** EUR 500K Pre-Seed (Valuation Cap: EUR 2.5M, Convertible Note/SAFE).
- **Founders:** 2 Co-Founders (Timu - CEO/Strategy/Marketing; Daria - COO/Operations/Content). 
- **The Gap:** There is NO technical co-founder yet. The early tech is built via agency/contractors. A full-time CTO will be hired at the Seed stage (Month 18+). 
- **Break-Even Target:** Month 12 (requiring ~145 paying subscribers).
- **Runway:** This EUR 500K represents roughly 24-33 months of runway including startup costs.
