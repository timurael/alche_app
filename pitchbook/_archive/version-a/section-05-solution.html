What is alche?

A premium wellness ecosystem that integrates a physical flagship experience, a proprietary mobile application, an outcome-based product brand, and a curated event programme into one data-enabled membership.

Four Pillars
01 Physical Experience
Berlin Mitte Flagship
Functional smoothies, LED red light therapy, curated product shelf, in-person events. The physical touchpoint that builds trust and anchors the brand in daily routine.
Generates behavioral data + walk-in conversions
02 Product Brand & Marketplace
Outcome-Based Blends
Proprietary blends (Glow, Energy, Calm, Recovery, Gut, Reset), curated third-party supplements and tools. Organized by outcome, not ingredient.
Product margins + replenishment revenue
03 Events & Culture
Community Programme
Wellness workshops, expert talks, community sessions. Each event strengthens retention, deepens brand loyalty, and generates engagement data.
Retention + engagement data
04 App & Membership
Digital Operating System
LED booking, digital menu, membership management, goal-based journeys, Glow Scan, progress tracking, Digital Twin. The layer that enables scale.
Recurring revenue + global scale
Integrated Information System
Each pillar generates data. The core information system aggregates it, enabling evidence-based decisions across the entire ecosystem.
Each pillar generates data → feeds the core information system → enables evidence-based decisions across the entire ecosystem. No single-vertical competitor can replicate this integration.
