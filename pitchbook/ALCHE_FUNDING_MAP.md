### 2.1 Forschungszulage (R&D Tax Credit)

- **Type**: Tax Credit (cash refund for loss-making companies)
- **Administering body**: BSFZ (certification) + local Finanzamt (payout)
- **Amount**: 35% of eligible R&D costs for SMEs. Max eligible costs EUR 10M/year (EUR 12M from 2026). For EUR 200K R&D spend = EUR 70K refund.
- **Equity/Dilution**: Non-dilutive (cash refund)
- **Eligibility**: Any German-taxable business. Any industry, any size, any stage. No minimum revenue. Pre-revenue startups get direct cash refund. Eligible R&D: algorithm development, behavioral science research, personalization engine, CGM integration. Routine updates and market research do NOT qualify. Retroactive to 1 January 2020.
- **Application**: Rolling, no deadline. Apply to BSFZ for certificate, then submit to Finanzamt. Can offset against quarterly tax prepayments from 2025.
- **URL**: https://www.innoscripta.com/en/about-us/blogs/a-step-by-step-guide-to-applying-for-germany-s-forschungszulage-r-d-tax-allowance
- **Fit for alche**: **HIGH** — Single highest-priority non-dilutive instrument. No partners, no consortium, no prototype, no traction needed. Just documented R&D activity. Cash refund even at zero revenue. Start immediately.
- **Action required**: (1) Begin documenting ALL R&D activities now (algorithm design, protocol research, platform architecture) with proper Frascati-standard records. (2) Engage a specialist (Innoscripta, SectorPi, or EurA AG) to review eligibility. (3) Submit BSFZ certificate once R&D is underway.
- **Blockers**: Requires actual qualifying R&D expenditure (employee/contractor costs). Pure marketing doesn't qualify.
