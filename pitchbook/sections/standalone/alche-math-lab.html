The Graveyard

Rest in Peace
Arivale
~$50M burned

Rest in Peace
Juicero
$120M raised
Proprietary hardware for a problem nobody had. DRM on juice bags. $400 machine replaced by human hands.
alche fix: Zero proprietary hardware. We're the software layer, not the device.
Rest in Peace
Noom (peak)
$3.7B → mass layoffs
Scaled too fast with paid acquisition. Couldn't retain at scale. Identity crisis between "wellness" and "medical."

Rest in Peace
DiGA Chasers
Regulatory death spiral
Chased German digital health certification (DiGA). Expensive, slow, locks revenue to insurance reimbursement.
alche fix: Consumer B2C. No DiGA certification. No insurance dependency.

Content-led, community-first, doesn't own hardware, doesn't chase reimbursement. ~$100M revenue.
What alche models: asset-light + content-first + community retention

Module 07

The Glossary

Every term investors will use in the meeting. Hover or tap to flip.

MRR
Monthly Recurring Revenue
hover to flip

The predictable revenue you get every month from subscriptions. Excludes one-time purchases. The single most important SaaS metric.

alche M12: EUR 7-8K
ARR
Annual Recurring Revenue
hover to flip

MRR times 12. This is the number used for valuation multiples. A SaaS at EUR 100K ARR with 8-15x multiple = EUR 800K-1.5M valuation.

alche M12: EUR 84-96K
ARPU
Average Revenue Per User
hover to flip

Total subscription revenue divided by number of paying subscribers. Higher ARPU = more value per customer = faster break-even.

alche target: EUR 49/mo
LTV
Lifetime Value
hover to flip

Total revenue from one subscriber over their entire relationship. LTV = ARPU x Gross Margin / Monthly Churn Rate. Must be at least 3x CAC.

alche: EUR 280-588
CAC
Customer Acquisition Cost
hover to flip

How much you spend to get one paying subscriber. Includes ads, content production, sales costs. Lower is better. Content-first = EUR 0 CAC initially.

alche blended: EUR 40-55
Churn
Monthly Churn Rate
hover to flip

% of subscribers who cancel each month. Industry average is 12-15% for wellness apps. 77% of health app users leave by Day 3.

alche target: 8%
SAFE
Simple Agreement for Future Equity
hover to flip

An investment instrument where investors give money now in exchange for equity later (at the next priced round). German equivalent: Wandeldarlehen (convertible loan).

alche: EUR 2.5M cap, 20% discount
COGS
Cost of Goods Sold
hover to flip

Direct costs to deliver the product. For SaaS: hosting, payment processing, API costs. Gross margin = Revenue minus COGS. Higher margin = more cash to grow.

alche sub COGS: EUR 2.50-3.50/user
CAGR
Compound Annual Growth Rate
hover to flip

The smoothed annual growth rate over a period. "The market grows at 3.3% CAGR" means it compounds 3.3% per year, every year.

German wellness: 3.3% CAGR
GMV
Gross Merchandise Volume
hover to flip

Total value of products sold through the platform. Different from revenue — if you sell EUR 10K of products at 60% margin, GMV is EUR 10K but revenue is EUR 6K.

Used for product commerce stream
TAM
Total Addressable Market
hover to flip

The total revenue opportunity if you captured 100% of the market. Global wellness = $6.3 trillion. Investors want to see the TAM → SAM → SOM cascade.

Global wellness: $6.3T
Runway
Cash Runway (months)
hover to flip

How many months you can operate before running out of money. Runway = Cash / Monthly Burn. Investors want 18-24 months minimum.

alche: 24 months
