Physical space: Phase 2 acquisition channel (seed-funded, not pre-seed)
The Berlin flagship in Mitte operates near breakeven standalone. It generates EUR 8,800-11,700/month in subscriber LTV through walk-in conversions and creates the environmental cues that bridge the 66-day habit formation gap digital-only tools structurally cannot. The physical space is an acquisition channel, not the business.
NOTE: The app is the operating system. The physical space is one revenue module. Every digital interaction feeds the KDGB loop. The architecture is invisible to the user -- what they experience is a single integrated platform.
