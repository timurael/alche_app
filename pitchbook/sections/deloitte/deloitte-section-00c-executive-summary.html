The Opportunity
The global wellness economy is $6.3T (GWI 2024). Germany spends EUR 495B on healthcare (12.3% GDP) with only 4.8% on prevention. No integrated longevity lifestyle platform exists in Europe. The competitive window is 12-18 months before US players localize.
The Solution
A subscription platform integrating biomarker tracking, personalized protocols, curated products, and community into one membership. Four-layer model (Know, Do, Get, Belong) creates revenue at every touchpoint. Subscription-first, physical space as acquisition channel.
Business Model
Three-tier subscription (EUR 19/49/99 per month). Weighted ARPU EUR 49. Conservative LTV:CAC ratio 5.1x. Revenue mix shifts from 52% subscription Y1 to 68% Y3. Digital margins 85-90%. 24-month runway with EUR 142K buffer at break-even.
Traction
Pre-product stage. 9 customer discovery interviews completed. 3 hypotheses invalidated (intellectual honesty signal). Content-first GTM running at zero CAC. Positioning validated: "longevity with intention." Medical advisor secured. Market research across 30+ competitors, 35+ research sessions.
