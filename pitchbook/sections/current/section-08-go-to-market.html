[08] Go-to-Market

Build the audience before the product.

Content-first. Community-first. The product comes to people who already trust the voice.

Most startups spend money to find customers. We find customers for free, then spend money to build what they already want. Our content-first strategy trades founder labor for capital: every video, newsletter, and community event builds an audience that converts directly into our waitlist, then our founding members, then our recurring revenue.

Two Flywheels, Sequenced
Flywheel 1 -- Running Now

The Content Engine

Create radical-honesty science content → attract health-curious audience → they engage and share → we learn what they need → create sharper content. No product needed. No funding needed.

Flywheel 2 -- Activated by Funding

The Product Engine

Know your body (data) → Do the protocols (action) → Get what you need (products) → Belong with others (community). Funding builds the product for an audience that already exists and trusts us.

Channel Strategy
Primary: Short-Form Video
TikTok + Instagram Reels -- @alche.space
Radical honesty science content. 3-5 posts per week. Pattern-breaking format: first piece is a collagen deep dive across 23 studies showing only industry-funded research is positive. This IS the brand voice.
Secondary: Newsletter
Weekly email -- Substack or Beehiiv
One deep dive per week. Captures emails -- the only owned asset. Social algorithms change; email lists compound. Where the most engaged followers live and conversion happens.
Amplifier: YouTube
Monthly "Alche Reviewed" long-form
10-20 minute deep research episodes. Evergreen SEO pillars that rank in search for years -- the moat no short-form competitor builds.
Amplifier: Berlin Events
Quarterly "Alche Salon" -- EUR 200-500/event
Science talks over wine at existing Berlin venues. One evening generates a month of content. Doubles as customer discovery. Biohackers Berlin meetup (2,213 members) is the warm audience.

Execution Roadmap

12 months to product-market fit.

Q1
M1-3
Foundation
Pre-funding // EUR 0 budget // Content engine ignition
  • Launch @alche.space. 3-5 short-form videos per week, honest science format.
  • Launch newsletter (Week 2). Goal: 300 subscribers by end of Q1.
  • 10 informal customer discovery conversations from social DMs and meetup contacts.
Q2
M4-6
Traction Proof
Pre-funding // EUR 200-500 events // Waitlist opens
  • First Alche Salon event. 30-40 people. Film everything -- one event produces 3-4 weeks of content.
  • Open waitlist landing page. Goal: 500 signups from newsletter + social + events.
  • Begin GmbH formation. Apply to GruendungsBONUS Plus (EUR 50K non-dilutive Berlin grant).
Q3
M7-9
Fundraise + Build
Funding closes // EUR 500K raise // MVP development begins
  • Pitch with real numbers: followers, newsletter subs, waitlist, event attendees.
  • Post-funding: hire technical co-founder / dev lead. Begin MVP development.
  • Events scale to monthly with 60-80 attendees.
Q4
M10-12
Beta Launch
Post-funding // Product in hands // Founding members
  • MVP beta to first 50-100 users from waitlist. Founding member pricing: EUR 19/month, locked for life.
  • Content evolves to include product teasers, beta user stories, "building in public" updates.
  • Physical space search begins. Berlin location planning for hybrid model proof.
