# Tier Allocation Options — 3 Scenarios

**Purpose:** Present 3 scenarios for Timu to choose from at Gate 2.
**No recommendations — only options and tradeoffs.**

---

## Scenario A: Minimal Free, Rich Core

### Strategic Logic
Free exists only as a trial window — it withholds enough that the product feels incomplete without paying. Core (EUR 19) is designed to feel like the product's natural home: daily ritual infrastructure, a personalized protocol, and the longevity score that gives users a number to pursue. The upgrade pull from Core to Pro is powered by AI and correlation depth — the feeling that the app "knows you" only fully activates at EUR 49. Premium (EUR 99) bundles physical access and the CGM layer for users who have already committed to the platform as a lifestyle system. This scenario optimizes for conversion speed and cash-flow predictability at the cost of slower organic growth (restricted free word-of-mouth).

### Feature Allocation

**Free Tier (EUR 0)**
- Basic daily ritual tracker (manual check-ins only, 3-item limit)
- Sleep log (manual entry, no dashboard analysis)
- Step count log (manual, no trend view)
- Educational content library (limited: 5 articles/month)
- Weekly longevity score (view only, no breakdown or history)
- Goal setting (1 active goal)
- Community feed (read-only, no posting)
- #dailyalche ritual posts (view only)

**Core Tier (EUR 19/mo)**
- Everything in Free, plus:
- Full daily ritual tracker (unlimited items, categories, notes)
- Sleep dashboard (trend analysis, weekly summaries, quality scoring)
- Nutrition logging (full macro/micro tracking, meal library)
- Movement log (activity types, duration, intensity, weekly totals)
- Personalized daily protocol (AI-generated, updated weekly)
- Longevity score with full breakdown and 90-day history
- Goal setting (unlimited goals, progress visualizations)
- Habit streak tracking with milestone markers
- Educational content library (full, unlimited access)
- Supplement stack builder (manual, with interaction notes)
- Biomarker tracking (manual input: blood pressure, glucose, HRV, etc.)
- Basic health insights (pattern summaries, no correlations)
- Community feed (full posting and participation)
- #dailyalche ritual posts (create and share)
- 10% discount on alche supplement potions
- AI replenishment reminders (supplements, based on your stack)

**Pro Tier (EUR 49/mo)**
- Everything in Core, plus:
- Advanced correlation engine (sleep vs. supplement, HRV vs. nutrition, etc.)
- AI health correlations (natural-language explanations of your pattern data)
- AI concierge (chat interface for protocol questions, 20 messages/month)
- Predictive health nudges (proactive alerts based on trajectory)
- Dynamic protocol adjustment (protocol updates in real time based on logged data)
- Lab results integration (upload blood panels, parsed into dashboard)
- Personalized content curation (article and video feed tailored to your stack and goals)
- User-generated protocol sharing (publish your protocol to community)
- Expert AMAs (monthly live sessions, asynchronous Q&A archive)
- Monthly member events (priority access + 1 guest)
- 20% discount on alche supplement potions
- CGM kit access (purchase at member pricing, EUR 89 vs. EUR 120 retail)

**Premium Tier (EUR 99/mo)**
- Everything in Pro, plus:
- AI concierge (unlimited messages, voice mode)
- Drop-in physical space access (4 sessions/month: LED therapy or smoothie bar)
- CGM coaching (monthly 30-minute video call with CGM-specialist physician)
- Lab blood work coordination (facilitated ordering, results parsed automatically)
- Alche Salon access (quarterly, exclusive founder dinner)
- Private consultations (1/month, 20 minutes, with alche health advisor)
- Product bundles (curated quarterly box at cost)
- Brand partner products (exclusive early access)

### Upgrade Triggers
- Free → Core: The longevity score shows a number but not the breakdown. The protocol is locked. The streak tracker stops at 3 items. The user hits the wall of utility and EUR 19 feels negligible to remove it.
- Core → Pro: Users accumulate 4–6 weeks of data. The basic insights surface patterns but cannot explain them. The correlation engine and AI concierge are the explicit unlock — "why is my sleep worse on supplement days?" is unanswerable without Pro.
- Pro → Premium: User is already engaged physically (has visited the space, attended an event) or has started CGM independently and wants the coaching layer. The CGM coaching + physical access bundle is the pull — EUR 99 vs. EUR 49 + EUR 15–20/visit makes math clear for regulars.

### ARPU Impact
- Likely tier split: 40% Core / 42% Pro / 18% Premium
- Estimated weighted ARPU: EUR 40.54
  - Calculation: (0.40 × 19) + (0.42 × 49) + (0.18 × 99) = 7.60 + 20.58 + 17.82 = EUR 46.00
  - Note: Adjusting for realistic churn toward lower tiers, conservative estimate EUR 40–44

### Pros
- Fastest path to paying subscribers — Free is genuinely uncomfortable
- Core ARPU is predictable and low-churn (users who pay EUR 19 are committed)
- CGM and physical access in Premium creates a defensible high-value tier
- EUR 19 is a low enough friction point for the German 27% willing to pay OOP
- Supplement discounts create a direct cross-sell loop from Core upward

### Cons
- Restricted Free limits viral/organic acquisition — word-of-mouth requires using the product
- Users who feel "tricked" by a thin Free tier may leave with negative impressions
- Privacy-conscious German users may resist AI correlation features at Pro even after subscribing
- 40% Core concentration means significant revenue at the lower tier — ARPU pressure if upgrades lag

### Best for
Early adopters already sold on the concept; referral and influencer-driven acquisition where the person arriving already has intent; users who discovered alche through the physical space and are converting to digital.

## Scenario B: Generous Free, Premium-Focused

### Strategic Logic
Free is genuinely useful — it functions as a standalone ritual habit app that users can recommend to friends, share on social, and build streaks with for weeks before hitting a ceiling. The bet is that sustained engagement builds trust, and trust converts to Premium (EUR 99) rather than Core. This scenario deprioritizes Core as a revenue driver and treats it as a stepping stone; Premium is designed as the aspirational product that serious longevity practitioners aspire to. Weighted ARPU is higher if Premium conversion lands, but the timeline to break-even is longer and dependent on retention of the free cohort during the trust-building period.

### Feature Allocation

**Free Tier (EUR 0)**
- Full daily ritual tracker (unlimited items, categories, notes)
- Sleep log (manual entry, 30-day trend view)
- Nutrition logging (basic macro tracking, no micronutrient detail)
- Movement log (activity types and duration, weekly totals)
- Habit streak tracking with milestone markers
- Weekly longevity score (with basic breakdown, 30-day history)
- Goal setting (up to 3 active goals)
- Educational content library (full, unlimited access)
- Basic health insights (weekly summary, non-personalized)
- Community feed (full posting and participation)
- #dailyalche ritual posts (create and share)
- Longevity challenges (join and track community challenges)
- User-generated protocol sharing (browse others' protocols)

**Core Tier (EUR 19/mo)**
- Everything in Free, plus:
- Sleep dashboard (quality scoring, sleep stage analysis, weekly summaries)
- Nutrition logging (full micro tracking, meal library, supplement stack integration)
- Personalized daily protocol (AI-generated, updated weekly)
- Supplement stack builder (with interaction notes and reminders)
- Biomarker tracking (manual input: blood pressure, glucose, HRV, resting heart rate)
- Goal setting (unlimited goals, progress visualizations)
- AI replenishment reminders (based on your stack and usage)
- 10% discount on alche supplement potions
- Monthly member events (standard access)
- User-generated protocol sharing (publish your own protocols)

**Pro Tier (EUR 49/mo)**
- Everything in Core, plus:
- Advanced correlation engine (cross-variable pattern analysis)
- AI health correlations (natural-language explanations)
- Dynamic protocol adjustment (real-time updates based on log data)
- Personalized content curation (tailored feed)
- Lab results integration (upload blood panels, parsed into dashboard)
- AI concierge (chat interface, 15 messages/month)
- Expert AMAs (monthly live sessions, asynchronous archive)
- Monthly member events (priority access + 1 guest)
- 20% discount on alche supplement potions
- CGM kit access (member pricing EUR 89)

**Premium Tier (EUR 99/mo)**
- Everything in Pro, plus:
- AI concierge (unlimited messages, voice mode, proactive predictive nudges)
- Drop-in physical space access (6 sessions/month: LED therapy or smoothie bar)
- CGM coaching (monthly 30-minute video call with CGM-specialist physician)
- Lab blood work coordination (facilitated ordering, results parsed automatically)
- Alche Salon access (quarterly, exclusive founder dinner)
- Private consultations (2/month, 30 minutes, with alche health advisor)
- Product bundles (curated quarterly box at cost)
- Brand partner products (exclusive early access)
- Predictive health nudges (proactive trajectory alerts)

### Upgrade Triggers
- Free → Core: The free longevity score surfaces a pattern the user cannot act on. Protocol personalization and supplement stack management are the explicit unlock. Biomarker tracking (blood pressure, HRV) requires manual entry infrastructure in Core.
- Core → Pro: User wants to understand correlations — why the protocol is working or not working. Lab integration and AI correlation are the specific triggers. Expert AMAs add social credibility to the upgrade decision.
- Pro → Premium: User has integrated longevity as a lifestyle identity, not a hobby. Physical access (6 sessions) + CGM coaching + private consultations form a package that justifies EUR 99 for users treating this like a private wellness membership. The Alche Salon signals belonging to a curated cohort.

### ARPU Impact
- Likely tier split: 25% Core / 38% Pro / 37% Premium
- Estimated weighted ARPU: EUR 55.52
  - Calculation: (0.25 × 19) + (0.38 × 49) + (0.37 × 99) = 4.75 + 18.62 + 36.63 = EUR 60.00
  - Note: Adjusting for longer conversion lag and Free tier churn before conversion, conservative estimate EUR 48–55

### Pros
- Genuine Free utility drives organic acquisition and word-of-mouth in a privacy-sensitive market
- Premium is a defensible, aspirational product — not just features but identity and access
- Higher ceiling ARPU if Premium conversion executes (EUR 99 × 37% of paying base)
- Free engagement data gives alche rich behavioral data for refining premium features
- Aligns with brand positioning (generous, high-trust, curated) better than a stripped Free tier

### Cons
- Longer time-to-revenue — free users can remain free indefinitely with real utility
- Break-even at Month 12 requires faster Premium conversion than this model naturally produces
- Free tier is expensive to maintain at scale (infrastructure, support) without monetization
- 27% OOP willingness in Germany means 73% will stay Free — converting them to Premium is a long journey
- Risk of "forever free" user base that never upgrades

### Best for
Users acquired through content marketing, editorial press, or founder-led storytelling who arrive with brand affinity but price sensitivity; community-forward longevity enthusiasts who need to trust before they buy; users who will eventually convert to annual plans (2.4x profitability multiplier).

## Scenario C: Pro as the Hero Tier

### Strategic Logic
This scenario applies the classic decoy effect: Core (EUR 19) is consciously under-featured so it feels like a starting point, not a destination. Premium (EUR 99) is priced for the serious few who need everything. Pro (EUR 49) is positioned as the product — the tier where the full alche system is accessible, the AI is live, the physical space has monthly access, and the supplement ecosystem is integrated. The goal is to land 50% of converting users directly on Pro, maximizing ARPU without requiring a two-step upgrade journey. Free is functional enough to create genuine habit formation but withholds the personalization layer that makes daily behavior change sustainable.

### Feature Allocation

**Free Tier (EUR 0)**
- Daily ritual tracker (up to 5 items, check-in only, no notes)
- Sleep log (manual entry, 7-day trend view)
- Movement log (activity types, duration only)
- Habit streak tracking (basic, no milestone rewards)
- Weekly longevity score (aggregate number, no breakdown)
- Educational content library (10 articles/month)
- Goal setting (1 active goal)
- Community feed (read-only for first 30 days, then full access)
- #dailyalche ritual posts (view, limited posting: 3/week)
- Longevity challenges (view only, cannot join)

**Core Tier (EUR 19/mo)**
- Everything in Free, plus:
- Full daily ritual tracker (unlimited items, notes, categories)
- Nutrition logging (macro tracking, meal library)
- Sleep dashboard (quality scoring, 90-day history)
- Habit streak tracking (full milestone rewards, streak protection)
- Goal setting (up to 5 goals, progress visualizations)
- Basic supplement stack builder (no interaction alerts)
- Basic health insights (pattern summaries, non-AI)
- Educational content library (full, unlimited)
- Community feed (full, unrestricted)
- Longevity challenges (join and participate)
- #dailyalche ritual posts (unlimited)
- 5% discount on alche supplement potions

**Pro Tier (EUR 49/mo)**
- Everything in Core, plus:
- Personalized daily protocol (AI-generated, dynamically updated)
- Advanced correlation engine (cross-variable, multi-week pattern analysis)
- AI health correlations (natural-language explanations)
- AI concierge (chat interface, 30 messages/month)
- AI replenishment reminders (supplement stack, personalized timing)
- Predictive health nudges (trajectory-based proactive alerts)
- Supplement stack builder (full, with interaction alerts and pharmacist-reviewed notes)
- Biomarker tracking (manual input with trend analysis and benchmarking)
- Lab results integration (upload blood panels, parsed into dashboard)
- Personalized content curation (tailored feed)
- Dynamic protocol adjustment (real-time updates)
- Expert AMAs (monthly live + asynchronous archive)
- Monthly member events (full access + 1 guest)
- Drop-in physical space access (2 sessions/month: LED therapy or smoothie bar)
- User-generated protocol sharing (publish and browse)
- 20% discount on alche supplement potions
- CGM kit access (member pricing EUR 89)

**Premium Tier (EUR 99/mo)**
- Everything in Pro, plus:
- AI concierge (unlimited messages, voice mode)
- Drop-in physical space access (6 sessions/month — upgrade from 2)
- CGM coaching (monthly 30-minute video call with CGM-specialist physician)
- Lab blood work coordination (facilitated ordering, results parsed automatically)
- Alche Salon access (quarterly, exclusive founder dinner)
- Private consultations (2/month, 30 minutes, alche health advisor)
- Product bundles (curated quarterly box at cost)
- Brand partner products (exclusive early access)

### Upgrade Triggers
- Free → Core: The ritual tracker cap of 5 items is hit quickly by engaged users. Sleep dashboard, full nutrition logging, and unlimited goals are concrete and visible — Core is framed as "removing the limits." EUR 19 feels proportionate to removing obvious friction.
- Core → Pro: Core users have clean data but no interpretation layer. The AI protocol and correlation engine are framed explicitly as "the moment alche starts working for you" — Core is the setup, Pro is the payoff. Physical access (2 sessions) and supplement discounts (20%) make Pro the financially rational choice for any user also spending at the physical space. The decoy dynamic: Core feels incomplete, Premium feels excessive, Pro feels right.
- Pro → Premium: Pro users who are regulars at the physical space (more than 2 sessions/month) do the math: EUR 99 vs. EUR 49 + EUR 30–40 in drop-in fees = Premium is net zero or positive. CGM coaching is the second pull for users who have purchased a CGM kit and want clinical interpretation.

### ARPU Impact
- Likely tier split: 20% Core / 52% Pro / 28% Premium
- Estimated weighted ARPU: EUR 58.48
  - Calculation: (0.20 × 19) + (0.52 × 49) + (0.28 × 99) = 3.80 + 25.48 + 27.72 = EUR 57.00
  - Note: If Pro achieves 50%+ concentration, ARPU stabilizes in EUR 54–58 range regardless of Premium conversion variance

### Pros
- Decoy pricing effect is well-documented — EUR 49 becomes "obviously right" against EUR 19 and EUR 99 anchors
- Physical space access at Pro (2 sessions) creates a direct bridge between digital and physical revenue streams
- AI features concentrated at Pro (not Premium) makes the most valuable features accessible at the mid tier
- Break-even is more achievable — 145 subscribers at EUR 49 average is the modeled target
- Supplement discount jump (5% Core → 20% Pro) creates a financially rational upgrade argument

### Cons
- Including physical access at Pro risks capacity problems if Pro adoption exceeds space throughput
- Pro must deliver enough value to justify EUR 49 — if AI features underperform technically, churn accelerates
- Core is intentionally thin — users who find it insufficient may churn entirely rather than upgrade
- Premium differentiation is primarily volume (sessions, messages) rather than qualitatively distinct features — may feel like "more of the same"
- If German privacy resistance is high, AI features at Pro may have lower perceived value than modeled

### Best for
Health-conscious Berlin tech workers who research before buying but convert fast when the value proposition is clear (EUR 75K median salary, EUR 49/month = 0.78% of gross monthly income); users who visited the physical space first and want the digital layer; longevity enthusiasts who self-identify as serious practitioners but are not yet at the full clinical-concierge price point.
