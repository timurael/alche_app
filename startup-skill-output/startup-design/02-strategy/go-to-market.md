# Go-to-Market Strategy -- Alche

> **Version:** 1.0 | March 2026 | Pre-validation

---

## GTM Philosophy

**Core principle:** The cafe is the top of funnel. Everything starts with a physical experience. Digital follows physical, not the other way around.

**Why physical-first in a digital world:**
- 90% wearable abandonment proves digital-only doesn't retain [Data]
- Community features reduce churn by 23% [Data]
- Physical spaces create emotional bonds and identity formation that apps cannot [Opinion]
- Berlin's longevity scene is thriving around physical experiences (ANTI, summits, meetups) [Data]
- The cafe generates revenue while acquiring customers -- making it a self-funding marketing channel [Hypothesis]

## Conversion Funnel Model

```
AWARENESS:    1,000 monthly (cafe + events + social + PR)
    |
    | 20% engage (200)
    ▼
CONSIDERATION: 200 monthly (event attendance, free app tier, cafe regulars)
    |
    | 15% convert (30)
    ▼
CONVERSION:   30 new subscribers/month
    |
    | 93-95% retained (monthly)
    ▼
RETENTION:    Net growth ~20-25 subscribers/month (after churn)
    |
    | 5 months to 145 subscribers (Phase 3 target)
```

**Honest assessment:** This funnel requires 1,000 monthly awareness touches, 200 engaged prospects, and 30 conversions. At a city of 3.8M, 1,000/month is tiny (0.03%). Achievable but requires consistent execution across multiple channels. The 15% consideration-to-conversion rate is optimistic; industry average for wellness subscriptions is 5-10%. At 10%, you need 50% more awareness. [Opinion]

## Flags

**RED:**
- The entire GTM depends on cafe-to-app conversion. If the physical space doesn't convert visitors to subscribers, there is no other high-volume, low-cost channel. Paid acquisition at EUR 80-150 CAC burns through EUR 500K in months.

**YELLOW:**
- 15% consideration-to-conversion is optimistic. Plan for 8-10% and model the implications.
- Berlin event calendar is concentrated in April-May. If cafe opening is Sept-Oct, there's a 4-month gap without major ecosystem events.
- CTO hire timeline affects app v2 delivery. If CTO isn't hired by Month 3, Phase 3 growth is delayed.
