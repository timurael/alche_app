## Runway Analysis

| Scenario | One-Time | Monthly Burn | EUR 500K Runway (no revenue) | With Base Revenue |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Lean | EUR 86K | EUR 23.5K | 17.6 months | ~24 months |
| Moderate | EUR 130K | EUR 33.5K | 11.0 months | ~18 months |
| Full | EUR 183K | EUR 44.5K | 7.1 months | ~14 months |

**Critical finding:** Only the Lean scenario achieves the stated 24-month runway with the EUR 500K raise. The Moderate scenario gives 18 months (with revenue). The Full scenario gives only 14 months -- dangerously short.

## Fixed vs. Variable Cost Split

| Type | Monthly (Lean) | % of Total | Scalability |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Fixed costs** | EUR 17,000 | 72% | Does not change with subscriber count |
| Rent | EUR 2,500 | | Fixed until lease renewal |
| Salaries (founders + CTO + dev) | EUR 13,000 | | Fixed |
| Admin/SaaS/legal | EUR 1,500 | | Fixed |
| **Variable costs** | EUR 6,500 | 28% | Scales with activity |
| Cafe operations | EUR 5,000 | | Scales with foot traffic |
| Marketing/events | EUR 1,500 | | Scales with growth goals |

**Implication:** 72% fixed cost base means high operating leverage. Once revenue exceeds fixed costs, every additional EUR is high-margin. But below break-even, costs are largely unchangeable.

## Cost Optimization Levers

| Lever | Savings | Trade-Off |
|---|---|---|
| Founder salaries to EUR 0 for 6 months | EUR 24,000 | Personal financial stress |
| CTO as equity-only for 6 months | EUR 30-60K | Harder to attract talent |
| Cafe in cheaper neighborhood | EUR 6-12K/yr | Less foot traffic, weaker brand signal |
| Shared kitchen/space arrangement | EUR 12-24K/yr | Less control over experience |
| Contractor developers instead of employees | EUR 10-20K/yr | Less commitment, potential quality issues |
| No-code/low-code MVP | EUR 15-25K | Technical debt, limited features |

## Flags

**RED:**
- Only the Lean scenario achieves 24-month runway. The original claim of "EUR 500K = 24 months" is true only with EUR 86K buildout, EUR 23.5K/mo burn, and growing revenue. Any cost overrun pushes this to 18 months or less.
- CTO hire at EUR 5K/mo (EUR 60K/yr) is significantly below Berlin market rate. This is the highest-risk line item in the budget.

**YELLOW:**
- 72% fixed costs create high operating leverage but also high risk pre-break-even. If revenue is slower than projected, there's limited ability to cut costs without cutting capability.
- Cafe buildout at EUR 40-80K range is wide. Must get actual contractor quotes before committing to investors.
