## Key Findings

### The Good News
1. **Market is real and growing.** $29-34B longevity market at 8-10% CAGR, validated by Function Health ($2.5B), ZOE ($500M+), Neko ($1.8B).
2. **Timing is exceptional.** GWS 2026 names over-optimization backlash as the defining trend. Women's longevity is a breakout category. Berlin is emerging as a European longevity hub.
3. **Positioning gap exists.** No brand combines science + joy + physical space + community in European longevity. ZOE is closest at 3.5/6 dimensions.
4. **Architecture is smart.** Asset-light, partnership-based model avoids every failure mode that killed $800M+ in longevity startups.

### The Hard Truths
1. **Zero customer validation.** Every customer-facing assumption is untested. Confidence on customer data: 2/10.
2. **Science credibility gap.** 0 papers vs. ZOE's 70+. This is the #1 investor objection.
3. **Break-even math is tight.** Only works in the lean burn scenario. Any cost overrun compresses runway below 18 months.
4. **Community in longevity is unproven.** No precedent at any scale. Fitness analogies may not transfer.
5. **EUR 500K = 24 months only if everything goes right.** Moderate burn = 18 months. Full burn = 14 months.

### The #1 Priority
**Run the validation playbook (EUR 10-12K, 8 weeks) BEFORE spending the raise.** 30 customer interviews + pricing study + 3-5 pop-up events will either confirm the thesis or reveal what needs to change. This is the highest-leverage activity available right now.

## Overall Assessment

**Score: 62/100 -- CONDITIONAL PROCEED**

The market opportunity is strong (8/10). The timing is excellent (8-9/10). The competitive position is defensible but has a critical science credibility gap. The customer validation is nearly nonexistent (2/10). The financial model works on paper but only in the lean scenario.

**Three actions that could raise the score to 76-87/100:**
1. Complete the validation playbook (+8-15 points)
2. Secure a CTO commitment (+3-5 points)
3. Sign one research partnership or advisory board (+3-5 points)

Cost: EUR 10-17K. Time: 8 weeks. This is the obvious next step.
