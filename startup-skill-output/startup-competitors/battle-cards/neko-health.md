# Battle Card: Neko Health

*Updated: 2026-03-12*

---

## Who They Are

Neko Health is a Stockholm-based AI-powered preventive body scanning company ($325M+ raised, $1.8B valuation) co-founded by Spotify's Daniel Ek. They offer a GBP 299 (~EUR 350) body scan that maps millions of health data points in minutes — checking moles, cardiovascular risk, metabolic syndrome, and prediabetes. 10,000+ scans completed, 100,000+ global waitlist, 80% rebooking rate. Currently in Stockholm, London, and Manchester, with New York planned for 2026.

---

## Strengths (Honest)

- **Founder pedigree:** Daniel Ek (Spotify founder) brings massive credibility and media attention. [Data]
- **Product-market fit:** 80% of patients book next scan before leaving — exceptional retention signal. [Data]
- **Waitlist demand:** 100,000+ people globally — demand far exceeds supply. [Data]
- **Capital position:** $325M+ raised at $1.8B valuation — well-funded for expansion. [Data]
- **AI diagnostics:** Detects changes invisible to naked eye — genuine technology differentiator. [Data]
- **Comprehensive scan:** Cardiovascular, dermatological, metabolic, and blood test analysis in one visit. [Data]

---

## Weaknesses (Specific)

- **Capital-intensive clinic model:** Each location requires significant buildout — slow to scale. [Assumption]
- **High price point:** GBP 299/scan limits accessibility to affluent consumers. [Data]
- **No ongoing digital product:** Between annual scans, no app, community, or daily engagement tool. [Data]
- **Limited locations:** 3 cities (Stockholm, London, Manchester) — tiny geographic footprint. [Data]
- **No community or lifestyle component:** Clinical experience, not a lifestyle brand. [Data]
- **No personalized follow-up:** Scan results + doctor consultation, but no ongoing coaching or support. [Data gap]
- **Wait times:** 100,000+ waitlist suggests months-long wait for appointments. [Assumption]

---

## How to Win Against Them

1. **Continuous vs. annual:** Neko gives you one scan per year. Alche gives you an ongoing longevity practice — daily app, monthly community, quarterly biomarker tracking.

2. **Accessible vs. premium:** EUR 350/scan is a luxury experience. Alche starts at EUR 19/month — longevity for everyone, not just the affluent.

3. **Community:** Neko is a clinical visit. Alche is a community you belong to. "Getting scanned is a moment. Living well is a practice."

4. **European footprint:** Neko is in Stockholm and London. Alche is in Berlin — different market, complementary positioning.

5. **Women's focus:** Neko is gender-neutral clinical. Alche is specifically designed for women 30-55.

---

## When They Win Over You (Honest)

- When the customer wants comprehensive AI body scanning — Alche doesn't offer this.
- When the customer values the Daniel Ek brand association.
- When the customer wants a clinical-grade preventive health assessment.
- When the customer is in Stockholm or London and wants a one-time comprehensive checkup.

---

## Key Vulnerability to Exploit

**Episodic, not continuous.** Neko is a yearly scan, not a daily practice. Between scans, there's nothing — no app, no community, no daily guidance. Alche fills the 364 days between scans. Neko and Alche could even be complementary: scan at Neko, live the results with Alche.
