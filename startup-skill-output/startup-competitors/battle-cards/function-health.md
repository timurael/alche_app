# Battle Card: Function Health

*Updated: 2026-03-12*

---

## Who They Are

Function Health is a US-based lab testing platform ($351M raised, $2.5B valuation) co-founded by celebrity doctor Mark Hyman. They offer 160+ biomarker tests twice yearly for $365/year, making advanced blood testing accessible to consumers without a doctor's referral. They've grown explosively from ~40,000 members in May 2024 to 200,000+ in May 2025, with an estimated ~$100M ARR (Feb 2025). They acquired Ezra for full-body MRI scanning and launched AI-powered health intelligence tools. US-only.

---

## Strengths (Honest)

- **Scale and velocity:** 200K+ members, 800 new subscribers/day in early 2025, 450% YoY growth. [Data]
- **Breadth of testing:** 160+ lab tests at $365/year is exceptional value vs. individual test costs ($1,200+ equivalent). [Data]
- **Founder brand:** Mark Hyman is one of America's most famous functional medicine doctors — 15 books, hit podcast. [Data]
- **Capital position:** $351M raised, $2.5B valuation gives massive runway. [Data]
- **Celebrity cap table:** Matt Damon, Zac Efron, Kevin Hart, Pedro Pascal = earned media machine. [Data]
- **Platform evolution:** Medical Intelligence Lab, ChatGPT integration, Ezra MRI, CT scans — becoming a longitudinal health intelligence platform. [Data]
- **Partnerships:** Equinox, NBPA, Thrive Global for B2B distribution. [Data]

---

## Weaknesses (Specific)

- **Data dump without guidance:** No doctor consultation included. Recommendations described as "so AI generated it's offensive" and "nothing personal." [Data]
- **Anxiety creation:** Multiple reports of users overwhelmed and anxious about results with no one to talk to. "A balance between empowerment and anxiety." [Data]
- **Second test bait-and-switch:** Only 25% of first-test biomarkers included in second test — many customers feel misled. [Data]
- **Geographic limitations:** US-only. Not available in NY/NJ without $248+ surcharges. [Data]
- **No community or lifestyle component:** Pure data platform with no behavioral, nutritional, or community support. [Data]
- **Cancellation complaints:** Non-refundable platform fees; one customer kept $132 of $365 on cancellation. [Data]
- **Cold, clinical positioning:** Appeals to quantified-self types but alienates the warm/inclusive longevity audience. [Opinion]

---

## How to Win Against Them

1. **Guidance vs. data dump:** Function gives you 160 numbers. Alche gives you a coach, a community, and a plan. "We don't just show you what's wrong — we help you make it right."

2. **European market:** Function Health has zero European presence. Alche serves the EU from day one via lab partnerships.

3. **Emotional wellbeing vs. anxiety:** Function's model can create health anxiety. Alche's "Imperfect Longevity" approach integrates results with community support, human connection, and a non-catastrophic framing.

4. **Community and lifestyle:** Function is a dashboard. Alche is a cafe, a community, and a daily practice. "Longevity isn't a lab report — it's a lifestyle."

5. **Women-centered:** Function's branding (Mark Hyman, Matt Damon, Kevin Hart) codes strongly male. Alche centers women 30-55 explicitly.

---

## When They Win Over You (Honest)

- When the customer primarily wants breadth and depth of lab testing at scale — 160+ biomarkers for $365/year is genuinely hard to beat on testing alone.
- When the customer is already a quantified-self enthusiast who wants raw data.
- When the customer is in the US and wants a data-first platform with growing AI capabilities.
- When the customer trusts Mark Hyman specifically.
