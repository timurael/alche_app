# Positioning Skill — Intake (Pre-Filled)

## Product
Alche is a longevity lifestyle platform: physical cafe/apothecary in Berlin + AI-powered subscription app (EUR 19/49/99/mo) + lab partnerships for biomarkers + community. "The Art of Curated Longevity." Translates longevity science into daily rituals you actually enjoy.

## Problem It Solves
Longevity brands are either science-deep but cold/male/exclusionary OR warm/inclusive but lacking scientific rigor. No brand integrates multi-dimensional longevity science with joyful, inclusive positioning — especially in Europe, especially for women.

## Current Alternatives
- ZOE — nutrition/gut science, warm brand, narrow scope
- Function Health — clinical biomarkers, cold/metric-obsessed
- AG1 — supplements, mass-market, science disputed
- Goop — lifestyle, science credibility problems
- Noom — behavioral/weight management, pivoting to longevity
- OURA/WHOOP — wearables, data-first, abandonment issues
- Gym membership — no science integration
- Nutritionist/doctor — expensive, no community
- Doing nothing / Huberman + supplements + annual blood test

## Best-Fit Customers
Women 30-55 in Berlin, wellness-engaged (>EUR 50/mo spend), longevity-curious but alienated by biohacker/optimization culture.
