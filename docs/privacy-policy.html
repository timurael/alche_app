2. What Data We Collect

Account data

  • Email address and name (for authentication and membership)
  • Membership status and booking history

Health and wellness data

  • Daily check-in responses (mood, energy, sleep quality)
  • Protocol tracking and completion status
  • Macro and nutrition logs
  • Biomarker entries (currently mock/demo data)

Camera and photo library

  • Camera access is used for Glow Scan, a skin analysis feature that captures images of your skin to provide visual feedback. Photos are processed locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to save or share them.
  • Photo library access allows you to select existing images for Glow Scan analysis.

Booking and event data

  • LED session bookings, smoothie orders, product browsing history, and event RSVPs

Usage analytics

  • Anonymous, aggregated usage data collected via TelemetryDeck (see Section 5)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalize the alche experience (bookings, protocols, recommendations)
  • To manage your membership and account
  • To process orders and reservations
  • To improve app features and fix bugs (via anonymous analytics only)
  • To communicate service-related updates

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third-party marketers.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data stays in the EU. Our backend runs on Supabase, hosted in Frankfurt, Germany (EU-West). Authentication, database, and file storage all reside within the European Union.

Local storage: Certain preferences and lightweight data (such as onboarding state, cached settings, and protocol progress) are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device unless synced to your account.

Camera and photos: Images captured or selected for Glow Scan are processed on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers unless a future feature explicitly requires it, in which case you will be asked for separate consent.

5. Analytics

We use TelemetryDeck for usage analytics. TelemetryDeck is a German company that is GDPR-native by design. It collects:

  • No personal data
  • No IP addresses
  • No device fingerprints
  • No cross-app tracking

TelemetryDeck provides us with aggregated, anonymous insights about how the app is used (e.g., which screens are visited most, general session duration). No individual user can be identified from this data.

6. Third-Party Tracking

alche does not integrate any third-party tracking SDKs. There are no advertising networks, no Facebook pixels, no Google Analytics, and no cross-app tracking identifiers in the app.

7. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): Processing your account, booking, and membership data is necessary to provide the service you signed up for.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): Camera and photo library access require your explicit permission, granted through iOS system prompts. You can revoke this at any time in your device Settings.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): Anonymous analytics via TelemetryDeck to improve the app, balanced against your privacy (no personal data is involved).

8. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data and receive a copy of it
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability -- receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent at any time (e.g., camera access via iOS Settings)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [INSERT EMAIL]. We will respond within 30 days as required by law.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Berlin Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information (Berliner Beauftragte fuer Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit).

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Anonymous analytics data (TelemetryDeck) cannot be traced back to you and is retained for product improvement purposes.

10. Children's Privacy

alche is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
